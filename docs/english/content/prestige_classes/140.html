:: Alaghar ::


(PRESTIGE CLASS)
Alaghor are dwarven priests of Clangeddin Silverbeard, Father of Battle, Lord of the Twin Axes, the Rock of Battle. As much warriors as priests, alaghor train tirelessly in their battle skills. Granted great power by Clangeddin, they are at the forefront of the dwarves in times of war, powerful battle leaders as well as gifted healers. Alaghor seek tirelessly to claim and create new magical weapons, particularly axes.

- Hit Die: d8
- Proficiencies: Alaghor are proficient with all simple and martial weapons, and with all armor and shields.
- Class Skills: Concentration, Craft(Armoursmith), Craft(Weaponsmith), Discipline, Heal, Intimidate, Lore, Persuade
- Skill Points: 2 + Int Modifier

Silverbeard: The alaghar's beard begins to turn to silver, granting a +1 natural armor bonus. This increase to +2 at level 5, and +3 at level 8

Clangeddin's Strike: While wielding a battleaxe, the alaghar may make an attack that, if it hits, will do triple damage on a successful hit. If the attack misses, the use is still lost.

Clangeddin's Might: While wielding a battleaxe, the alaghar may invoke the might of Clangeddin, gaining an extra attack for a number of rounds equal to his Constitution modifier.

Rockburst: You create a burst of rock that deals damage to creatures and objects it strikes. Make a ranged touch attack against each object in the area, doing 1d4+1 points of bludgeoning damage per Alaghar level, to a maximum of 10d4+10.
