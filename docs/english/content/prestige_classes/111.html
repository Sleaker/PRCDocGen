:: Favored of Milil ::


(PRESTIGE CLASS)
While many performing bards worship Milil, few of them have the religious devotion to become one of his clergy. However, all of the leaders of his church were also trained as musicians in some form or another. While this does not mean all of them have pursued the path of the bard, those that have often hold positions of greater authority in the church. A few of these Milil selects for his own members with enough spiritual tenacity and ability in the musical arts to attract his favor. These Favored of Milil are renowned for their musical ability, because of its divine presence. Not only does their music cause inspiration in their allies, the very notes are laced with divine power granted by Milil himself.

- Hit die: d6
- Proficiencies: The Favored of Milil gains no additional weapon or armor proficiencies
- Skill points per level: 4 + Int modifier

REQUIREMENTS

Alignment: Neutral Good, Chaotic Good, or True Neutral
Skills: Perform 14 ranks, Lore 14 ranks
Spellcasting: Ability to cast 4th level Divine spells and 2nd level Arcane spells
Feats: Extra Music
Special: Must worship Milil (Clerics must have two of following domains: Good, Knowledge, Nobility or Charm)

CLASS SKILLS

Bluff, Concentration, Discipline, Heal, Lore, Perform, Persuade, Spellcraft, Tumble, Use Magic Device (Cross-Class)

CLASS FEATURES

Base Attack: +3/4 per level, rounded down
Saving Throws: Fortitude weak, Will strong, Reflex strong

Level
1: Divine Song I
2: Encore Performance
3: Divine Song II
5: Divine Song III
7: Divine Song IV
9: Divine Song V
10: Greater Divine Song

Divine Song I: A Favored of Milil is able to sing a bard song that has the effects of divine spells. The Favored of Milil may select one of the following first-level divine spells as a Divine Song: Bless, Conviction, Cure Light Wounds, Remove Fear, Sanctuary, Shield of Faith.

Divine Song II: The Favored of Milil may select one of the following second-level divine spells as a Divine Song: Aid, Bull's Strength, Cure Moderate Wounds, Eagle's Splendor, Endurance, Fox's Cunning, Lesser Restoration, Owl's Wisdom. Alternatively, he may select a lower-level Divine Song instead.

Divine Song III: The Favored of Milil may select one of the following third-level divine spells as a Divine Song: Clairaudience/Clairvoyance, Clarity, Cure Serious Wounds, Negative Energy Protection, Prayer, Protection from Elements, Remove Curse. Alternatively, he may select a lower-level Divine Song instead.

Divine Song IV: The Favored of Milil may select one of the following fourth-level divine spells as a Divine Song: Cure Critical Wounds, Death Ward, Freedom of Movement, Panacea, Restoration, Stoneskin, True Seeing. Alternatively, he may select a lower-level Divine Song instead.

Divine Song V: The Favored of Milil may select one of the following fifth-level divine spells as a Divine Song: Monstrous Regeneration, Raise Dead, Spell Resistance. Alternatively, he may select a lower-level Divine Song instead.

Encore Performance: The Favored of Milil can add 1/2 his class level to his bard song uses, once per day.

SPELLCASTING

Upon gaining a level in Favored of Milil, the character gains new spells per day as if he had also gained a level in his highest divine caster class.
