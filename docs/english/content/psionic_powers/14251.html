:: Genesis ::


Metacreativity (Creation)
Level: Shaper 9
Manifesting Time: 1 standard action
Range: Close
Target: A location for the portal
Effect: A demiplane and a portal to it
Duration: Instantaneous
Saving Throw: None
Power Resistance: No
Power Points: 17
Metapsionics: None

You create a finite plane with limited access: a Demiplane. Demiplanes created by this power are very small, very minor planes, but they are private. Upon manifesting this power, a portal will appear in front of you that the manifester and all party members may use to enter the plane. Once anyone exits the plane, the plane is shut until the next manifesting of this power. Exiting the plane will return you to where you cast the portal.
