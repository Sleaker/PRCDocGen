:: Moment of Prescience, Psionic ::


Clairsentience
Level: Psion/wilder 7
Manifesting Time: 1 swift action
Range: Personal
Target: You
Duration: 1 round
Power Points: 13
Metapsionics: Extend

You add a bonus equal to your manifester level (max +25) to attack, armour class, saves, or skills for the duration of the power.

Manifesting the power is a swift action, like manifesting a quickened power, and it counts toward the normal limit of one quickened power per round.
