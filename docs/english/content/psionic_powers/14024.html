:: My Light ::


Psychokinesis [Light]
Level: Psion/wilder 1, psychic warrior 1
Manifesting Time: 1 standard action
Range: Personal
Effect: 20 m spread of light emanating from you
Duration: 10 min./level
Power Points: 1
Metapsionics: Extend

When you manifest this power, your body begins to glow with bright white light, allowing you and others to see clearly even in the night.
