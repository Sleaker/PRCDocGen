:: Telekinetic Maneuver ::


Psychokinesis [Force]
Level: Psion/wilder 4
Manifesting Time: 1 standard action
Range: Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./ level)
Target: One creature
Duration: 1 round / 2 levels
Saving Throw: None
Power Resistance: Yes
Power Points: 7
Metapsionics: Extend, Twin

You mentally push a foe, attempting to knock it prone and disarm it. The DC of the discipline check for the target to resist being knocked down or disarmed is equal to your manifester level + you ability modified in your manifesting stat. The Discipline checks to avoid being knocked down and disarmed are rolled separately.

Augment: For every 2 additional power points spent, the DC of the Discipline check is increased by 1.
