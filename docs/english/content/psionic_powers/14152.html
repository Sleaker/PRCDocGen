:: Freedom of Movement, Psionic ::


Psychoportation
Level: Psion/wilder 4, psychic warrior 4
Manifesting Time: 1 standard action
Range: Personal
Target: You
Duration: 10 min./level
Power Points: 7
Metapsionics: Extend

This power enables you to move and attack normally for the duration of the power, even under the influence of magic that usually impedes movement, such as paralysis, solid fog, slow, and web.
