:: Dismissal, Psionic ::


Psychoportation
Level: Nomad 4
Manifesting Time: 1 standard action
Range: Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
Target: One extraplanar creature
Duration: Instantaneous
Saving Throw: Will negates
Power Resistance: Yes
Power Points: 7
Metapsionics: Twin

This spell forces an extraplanar creature back to its home plane if it fails a save. Extraplanar creatures are outsiders and elementals. This spell does not work on summons that are not of these races.
