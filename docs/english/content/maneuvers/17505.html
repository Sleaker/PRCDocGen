:: Tactical Strike ::


White Raven (Stance)
Level: Crusader 3, Warblade 3
Initiation Action: 1 Swift Action
Range: 10ft.
Area: 10 ft radius centred on you.
Duration: Stance.

You shout orders that help coordinate your allies' efforts. They harass their enemies, shield each other from attacks. and otherwise maximize the support they lend to each other.

All allies gain a bonus while flanking equal to +1/2 per your initiator level.
