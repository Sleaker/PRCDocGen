:: Rallying Strike ::


Devoted Spirit (Strike)
Level: Crusader 6
Prerequisite: Two Devoted Spirit maneuvers.
Initiation Action: 1 Standard Action
Range: Melee Attack
Target: One Creature

Your weapon blazes with divine energy as you smite your enemy. The energy discharges in a great pulse, sweeping over your allies and mending their wounds.

You make a single attack against an enemy who's alignment has at least one component different from yours. If you hit, you and all allies within 30 feet is healed 3d6 + 1 per initiator level (max of +15).
