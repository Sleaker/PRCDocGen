:: Hamstring Attack ::


Tiger Claw (Strike)
Level: Swordsage 7, warblade 7
Prerequisite: Three Tiger Claw maneuvers
Initiation Action: 1 standard action
Range: Melee attack
Target: One creature
Saving Throw: Fortitude half
Duration: 1 minute

You slice into your opponent's legs, leaving him hobbled and stumbling about.

As part of this maneuver, you make a single melee attack. If this attack hits, it deals damage as normal. In addition, the target takes 1d8 points of Dexterity damage and a -10 foot penalty to speed for 1 minute. A successful Fortitude save (DC 17 + your Str modifier) halves both the Dexterity damage and the speed penalty.
