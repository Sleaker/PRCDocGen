:: Burning Brand ::


Desert Wind (Boost)
Level: Swordsage 2
Initiation Action: 1 Swift action
Range: Personal
Target: You
Duration: End of Turn

Your weapon transforms into a roaring gout of flame. As you swing your burning blade, it stretches out beyond your normal reach to scorch your foes.

You take a full attack action, with your reach increasing by 5 feet. Your weapon deals fire damage instead of normal damage for this round.
This is a supernatural maneuver.
