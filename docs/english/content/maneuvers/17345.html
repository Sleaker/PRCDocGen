:: Radiant Charge ::


Devoted Spirit (Strike) [Good]
Level: Crusader 5
Prerequisite: One Devoted Spirit maneuver
Initiation Action: 1 Full-round action
Range: Melee
Target: One creature
Duration: 1 round

You gather the power of your faith and discipline, surrounding yourself in an aura of blinding glory.

You make a charge attack. If the target is evil aligned, you gain 6d6 damage on the attack, and damage reduction of 10/- for one round.
