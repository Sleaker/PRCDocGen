:: Shadow Garrote ::


Shadow Hand (Strike)
Level: Swordsage 3
Prerequisite: One Shadow Hand maneuver
Initiation Action: 1 Standard Action
Range: 60'
Target: One Living Creature
Duration: See text.
Save: Fortitude partial.

With a subtle gesture, you carve a slice of shadow and cast it towards your foe. It wraps around the creature's neck and squeeze the life from it.

If successful on a ranged touch attack, you deal 5d6 damage. It must make a Fortitude save or be flat-footed for one round.
This maneuver is a supernatural maneuver.
