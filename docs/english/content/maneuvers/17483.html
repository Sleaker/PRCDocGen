:: Rabid Wolf Strike ::


Tiger Claw (Strike)
Level: Swordsage 2, Warblade 2
Initiation Action: 1 Standard Action
Range: Melee Attack
Target: One Creatures

You foam at the mouth and scream in rage as you make a powerful attack against your enemy. You set aside all thoughts of defense as you lunge forward.

Make a single attack with a +4 bonus on the attack roll, and +2d6 damage. You take a -4 penalty to AC for the rest of the round.
