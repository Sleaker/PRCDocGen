:: Shadow Blade Technique ::


Shadow Hand (Strike)
Level: Swordsage 1
Initiation Action: 1 Standard Action
Range: Melee Attack
Target: One Creature

You weave your weapon in an elaborate pattern, creating an illusory double that glows with white energy. As you make your attack, both you true weapon and the illusion slash at your foe.

You roll to attack twice, once for the normal blade and once for the double. The normal blade deals standard damage. If the illusion hits, it deals 1d6 cold damage.
This is a supernatural maneuver.
