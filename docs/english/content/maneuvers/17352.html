:: Tide Of Chaos ::


Devoted Spirit (Strike) [Chaos]
Level: Crusader 5
Prerequisite: One Devoted Spirit maneuver
Initiation Action: 1 Full-round action
Range: Melee
Target: One creature
Duration: 1 round

The power of chaos swirls around you, lending strength to your attacks as you cast your fate to the whims of luck.

You make a charge attack. If the target is law aligned, you gain a +8 bonus on your attack and 4d6 damage. If it hits, you gain total concealment for one round.
