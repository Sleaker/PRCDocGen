:: Song of the White Raven ::

Type of Feat: General
Prerequisite: Bardic music, one White Raven maneuver
Specifics: While you are in any White Raven stance, you can activate your bardic music as a swift action. Your crusader and warblade levels stack with your bard levels to determine the bonus granted.
Use: Selected

Requires All of These Feats:

Bard Song
