:: Psionic Weapon ::

Type of Feat: Psionic
Prerequisite: Str 13
Specifics: To use this feat, you must expend your psionic focus. Your attack with a melee weapon deals an extra 2d6 points of damage. If your attack misses, you still expend your psionic focus.
Use: Selected

Requires One of These Feats:

Psionic Focus
Wild Talent

Feat Replaced By:

Greater Psionic Weapon

Required For the Following Feats:

Deep Impact
Greater Psionic Weapon
