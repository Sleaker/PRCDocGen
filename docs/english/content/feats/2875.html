:: Extraordinary Artisan I ::

Type of Feat: General
Prerequisite: Any item creation feat.
Specifics: You are an expert at creating magic items at a lower cost than usual. When determining the gold piece cost in raw materials you need to craft any item, reduce the base price by 25%. You can gain this feat multiple times. Its effects stack (geometrically).
Usage: Automatic.

Feat Replaced By:

Extraordinary Artisan II

Required For the Following Feats:

Extraordinary Artisan II
