:: Wild Talent ::

Type of Feat: General
Prerequisites: None
Specifics: Your latent power of psionics flares to life, conferring upon you the designation of a psionic character. As a psionic character, you gain a reserve of 2 power points and can take psionic feats and metapsionic feats. You do not, however, gain the ability to manifest powers simply by virtue of having this feat.
Use: Automatic

Required For the Following Feats:

Invest Armour
Mental Leap
Narrow Mind
Psionic Dodge
Psionic Fist
Psionic Meditation
Psionic Shot
Psionic Talent I
Psionic Weapon
Speed Of Thought
Wounding Attack
