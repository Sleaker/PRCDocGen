:: Tempest Breath ::

Type of Feat: Metabreath
Prerequisite: Str 13, Power Attack, breath weapon with a recharge time

When activated, your breath weapon increases in force, acting like a strong wind on those within. Affected creatures must roll a Fort save or be knocked down. The DC of the save depends solely on your size, starting at 15 for Large. This knockdown effect also only works on those 2 or more size categories smaller than you.
This increases your recharge time by one round when used.
Use: Selected.

Requires All of These Feats:

Power Attack
