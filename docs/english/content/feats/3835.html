:: Blade Meditation: Tiger Claw ::

Type of Feat: General
Prerequisites: Concentration 1, Base Attack Bonus +4, One Maneuver
Specifics: When you take this feat, choose a discipline. You gain a +1 bonus on damage rolls with all the preferred weapons of your chosen discipline when using a strike combat maneuver, as well as a +2 bonus on checks involving the discipline's key skill. The save DCs of any maneuvers that you perform from the chosen discipline are increased by I, if they have a save DC.
