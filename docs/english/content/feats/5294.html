:: Stone Power ::

Type of Feat: General
Prerequisite: Str 13, one Stone Dragon maneuver
Benefit: You can take a penalty of as much as -5 on your attack rolls. This number cannot exceed your base attack bonus. You gain temporary hit points equal to twice the number that you subtract from your attack rolls (to a maximum of +10). These temporary hitpoints last until the beginning of your next turn.

Pressing the feat repeatedly will cycle through the options available.
Use: Selected.
