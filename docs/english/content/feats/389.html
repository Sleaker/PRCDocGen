:: Expertise ::

Type of Feat: General
Prerequisite: Int 13+.
Required for: Improved Expertise, Whirlwind Attack
Specifics: A character with this feat can make defensive attacks, gaining a +5 bonus to AC but receiving a -5 penalty to attack rolls.
Use: Combat Mode.
