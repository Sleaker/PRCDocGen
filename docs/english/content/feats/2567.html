:: Ethran ::

Type of Feat: Class.
Prerequisite: Female*, Charisma 11+, Spellcaster
Specifics: Gain plus 2 bonus to checks on Animal Empathy and Charisma-based skills.
Use: Automatic.

(*If you take this feat as a male, you will be asked to retake your feats. If you take this feat as a first level male, you will be unable to level up.)
