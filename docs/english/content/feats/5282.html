:: Profane Agony ::

Type of Feat: General
Prerequisite: Turn Undead, Death or Destruction domain
Benefit: You can spend a rebuke undead attempt as a full-round action to channel your deity's displeasure at a single living creature within 30 feet. That creature takes 3d6 points of damage and is sickened for 1 round. A successful Fortitude save (DC 10 + one-half your level + your Cha modifier) negates the damage, but not the sickening effect.
Use: Selected

Requires All of These Feats:

Turn Undead

Requires One of These Feats:

Death Domain Powers
Destruction Domain Powers
