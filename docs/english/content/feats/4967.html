:: Lingering Breath ::

Type of Feat: Metabreath
Prerequisite: Con 15, Clinging Breath, breath weapon with a recharge time

When activated, your breath weapon lasts for a time after you stop breathing. Until the selected duration ends, your breath persists in a cloud identical in size and shape to your normal breath, continuing to affect those within. Those in the cloud must roll a save against each round of breath, until they leave that area.
If used with Clinging Breath, only the initial round of the breath clings.
This increases your recharge time by two rounds for each round of duration when used.
Use: Selected.
