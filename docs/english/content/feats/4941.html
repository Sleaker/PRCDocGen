:: Split Psionic Ray ::

Type of Feat: Metapsionic
Prerequisites: Any other metapsionic feat
Specifics: To use this feat, you must expend your psionic focus. You can split psionic rays you manifest. The split ray affects any two targets that are both within the power's range and within 30 feet of each other. If the ray deals damage, each target takes as much damage as a single target would take.
Using this feat increases the power point cost of the power by 2. The power's total cost cannot exceed your manifester level.
Use: Selected

Requires All of These Feats:

Psionic Focus
