:: Chosen of Iborighu ::

Type of Feat: General
Prerequisites: Must be Chaotic Evil
Specifics: Once per day, you can cause one creature within 30 feet that meets your gaze to become overwhelmed with a wave of supernatural cold. The target can resist the effects of this attack with a successful Fortitude save (DC 10 + 1/2 your character level + your Charisma modifi er), otherwise it causes cold damage equal to your Charisma modifi er + 3 (minimum 1 point of damage) and causes the victim to become fatigued. If you use this attack against someone who is fatigued, they instead become exhausted. Using this supernatural ability is a standard action that does not provoke attacks of opportunity.
Use: Selected
