:: Improved Bullrush ::

Type of Feat: General
Prerequisite: Str 13, Power Attack
Specifics: When you perform a bull rush you do not provoke an attack of opportunity from the defender. You also gain a +4 bonus on the opposed Strength check you make to push back the defender.
Use: Automatic

Requires All of These Feats:

Power Attack

Required For the Following Feats:

Hammer's Edge
Type of Feat: General Prerequisite: Two-Weapon Fighting, Weapon Focus (halberd) Benefit: When you make a full attack with your halberd, you gain a +1 dodge bonus to your Armor Class as well as an additional attack with the weapon at a -5 penalty. This attack deals points of bludgeoning damage equal to 1d6 + 1/2 your Strength modifier. Use: Selected
