:: Tiger Blooded ::

Type of Feat: General
Prerequisite: Ability to rage, wild shape, or shift; one Tiger Claw maneuver
Benefit: While you are in a rage, shifted, or wild shaped, you attempt to knock back 5 feet a creature of your size category or smaller that you hit with a Tiger Claw strike unless it succeeds on a Fortitude save (DC 10+1/2 your character level + your Str modifier).
Use: Automatic.
