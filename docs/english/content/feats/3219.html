:: Sanctify Martial Strike (Kama) ::

Type of Feat: General.
Prerequisite: Weapon Focus with chosen weapon, Charisma 15, Good Alignment*.
Specifics: The weapon chosen deals +1 damage vs. Evil, +1d4 vs. Outsider and Undead.

(*Note: You can select this feat even when evil or neutral, but it will not give you any benefits, so be smart and don't choose it if you can't use it.)

Use: Automatic.

Requires All of These Feats:

Weapon Focus (kama)
