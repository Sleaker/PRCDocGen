:: Lolth's Meat ::

Type of Feat: General
Prerequisite: Drow (Elf).
Specifics: If you kill a living creature that has an Intelligence score of 3 or higher with a melee attack, you gain a +1 morale bonus on attack rolls, damage rolls, and saving throws for 1 round per Character Level.
Use: Automatic.

Warning: If you take this class without the race of Elf it will not function, it will be a wasted feat.

Requires All of These Feats:

Hardiness vs. Enchantments
Immunity To Sleep

Requires One of These Feats:

Light Blindness
Partial Skill Affinity (Search)
Weapon Proficiency (Elf)
