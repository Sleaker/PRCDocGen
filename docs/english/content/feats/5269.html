:: Haunting Melody ::

Type of Feat: General
Prerequisite: Bardic Music, Perform 9
Benefit: You can inspire fear in enemies within 30 feet of you. Any opponent in range must succeed on a Will saving throw (DC 10 + 1/2 your bard level + your Cha modifier) or become shaken for a number of rounds equal to your ranks in the Perform skill. This is a fear effect.
Use: Automatic

Requires All of These Feats:

Bard Song
