:: Wounding Attack ::

Type of Feat: Psionic
Prerequisites: Base attack bonus +8
Specifics: To use this feat, you must expend you psionic focus. You can make an attack with such vicious force that you wound your opponent. A wound deals 1 point of Constitution damage to your foe in addition to the usual damage dealt.
If you miss, you still expend your psionic focus.
Use: Selected

Subradial entries:

Wounding Attack - Melee
Wounding Attack - Ranged

Requires One of These Feats:

Psionic Focus
Wild Talent
