:: Profane Lifeleech ::

Type of Feat: Divine
Prerequisite: Ability to rebuke undead.
Benefit: As a standard action, you can spend two of your rebuke attempts to deal 1d6 points of damage to all living creatures within a 30-foot burst. You are healed of an amount of damage equal to the total amount of hit points that you drain from affected creatures, but this healing does not allow you to exceed your full normal hit point total.

Special: This feat deals no damage to constructs or undead.

Requires All of These Feats:

Turn Undead
