:: High Sword Low Axe ::

Type of Feat: General
Prerequisite: Improved Trip, Two-Weapon Fighting, Weapon Focus (battleaxe, handaxe, or dwarven waraxe), Weapon Focus (bastard sword, long sword, scimitar)
Benefit: If you hit the same creature with both your sword and your axe in the same round, you may make a free trip attempt against that foe.
Use: Selected

Requires All of These Feats:

Improved Trip
Two-Weapon Fighting
