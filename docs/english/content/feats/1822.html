:: Shifter Savagery ::

Type of Feat: Shifter
Prerequisite: Shifter, Gorebrute, Longtooth, or Razorclaw trait, +6 BAB, ability to rage/frenzy
Your natural weapon increases 2 sizes and your critical range with your natural weapon doubles while shifting and raging.
Use: Automatic.

Requires All of These Feats:

Barbarian Rage

Requires One of These Feats:

Gorebrute
Longtooth
Razorclaw
