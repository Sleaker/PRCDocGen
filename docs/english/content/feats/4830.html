:: Unavoidable Strike ::

Type of Feat: Psionic
Prerequisites: Str 13, Psionic Fist, base attack bonus +5
Specifics: To use this feat, you must expend you psionic focus. You can resolve your unarmed strike or attack with a natural weapon as a touch attack.
If you miss, you still expend your psionic focus.
Use: Selected

Requires All of These Feats:

Psionic Fist
