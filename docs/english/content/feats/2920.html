:: Craft Construct ::

Type of Feat: General
Prerequisite: Craft Magic Arms and Armor, Craft Wondrous Item
Specifics: Allows the user to craft constructs.
Usage: Automatic. Use the Examine Recipe and Craft Item once to examine a recipe and a second time within 6 seconds to craft the item.

Requires All of These Feats:

Craft Magic Arms and Armor
Craft Wondrous Item
