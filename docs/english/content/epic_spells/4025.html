:: Epic Spell: Eternal Freedom ::


School: Abjuration
Components: V,S
Range: Touch
Target: Creature touched
Duration: Permanent
Saving Throw: None (harmless)
Spell Resistance: No (harmless)

The subject boldly goes where others fear to tread, becoming permanently immune to the following specific spells, effects, and spell-like abilities: paralysis, entangle, hold person, hold monster, hold animal, slow, stun, and web.
