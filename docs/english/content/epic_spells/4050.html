:: Epic Spell: Pestilence ::


School: Necromancy
Components: V,S
Range: Personal
Area: 40-ft.-radius
Duration: Instantaneous
Saving Throw: Fortitude negates
Spell Resistance: Yes

When Pestilence is successfully cast, a wave of illness radiates outward from you, instantly infecting every living creature in the area with the debilitating disease known as slimy doom.
