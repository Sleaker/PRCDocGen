:: Ability Increase ::

Type of Feat: Class
Prerequisite: Dragon Devotee 1

Over time the Dragon Devotee becomes more dragonlike gaining the following boosts to their abilities:
1 - +2 Charisma
3 - +2 Constitution
5 - +2 Strength

These bonuses do not stack with those provided by the Dragon Disciple class, as they are part of the same template chain.
