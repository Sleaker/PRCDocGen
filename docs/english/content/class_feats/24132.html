:: Tactical Insight (Ex) ::

Type of Feat: Class Specific
Prerequisites: Eternal Blade level 8
Specifics: Although you may be young by the elve's reckoning, your blade guide lends you the experience and wisdom of one who has fought battles across countless fields.
For the rest of your turn, any opponent you hit with a melee attack takes a penalty to AC equal to your Intelligence bonus (if any) for 1 round.
If you lose access to your blade guide, you lose this ability until it returns.
Use: Selected
