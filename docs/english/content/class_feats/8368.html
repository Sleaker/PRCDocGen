:: Swordsage Roots Of The Mountain ::

Stone Dragon (Stance)
Level: Crusader 3, Swordsage 3, Warblade 3
Initiation Action: 1 Swift Action
Range: Personal
Target: You
Duration: Stance

You crouch and set your feet flat on the ground, rooting yourself to the spot you stand. Nothing can move you from this place.

You gain a +10 bonus on all ability checks for grapples, trips, overruns and bull rushes. Any creature that attempts to move past you gains a -10 penalty to Tumble, and you gain DR 2/-.
This stance ends if you move more than 5 feet for any reason.
