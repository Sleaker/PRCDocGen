:: Distract ::

Type of Feat: Class Specific
Prerequisite: Shadowmind level 1.
Specifics: Add the Distract power to the Shadowmind's power list, if he did not know it already. Once per day, the Shadowmind can manifest Distract at a reduced power point cost. The cost is reduced by 1 point per Shadowmind level, to a minimum of 1. This does not allow you to exceed the cap of power points you may spend on a power.
Use: Selected.
