:: Gorebrute ::

Type of Feat: Shifter Trait
Prerequisite: Shifter
You can "shift" and assume aspects of your ancestral heritage.
Gorebrute shifters gain +2 to Strength, and grow horns that do 2d6 damage on a charge, when they shift.
Use: Automatic.

Required For the Following Feats:

Gorebrute Elite
Shifter Savagery
