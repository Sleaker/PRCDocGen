:: Warblade Step Of The Wind ::

Setting Sun (Stance)
Level: Swordsage 1
Initiation Action: 1 Swift Action
Range: Personal.
Target: You.
Duration: Stance.

You walk across rubble and other broken terrain with deceptive ease, allowing you to take advantage of your opponents as they struggle to move at full speed.

You become immune to movement speed decreases and being slowed, and gain a +2 bonus to attack and a +4 bonus on Bull Rush attempts, both to attack and to defend.
