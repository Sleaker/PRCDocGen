:: Third Hand ::

Type of Feat: Class
Prerequisite: Lasher level 2.
Specifics: At 2nd level, a lasher's precision with the whip allows her to use it almost like a third hand - a third hand at the end of a flexible 15-foot-long arm - as a standard action. A lasher successfully performs a task if her attack roll equals or exceeds the DC for a given task:

Punch a button, snuff a candle flame, flick a coin lying on the ground, etc.
Range 15 feet, DC 15.

Retrieve an unattended object of up to 20 pounds, and deposit into your inventory.
Range 15 feet, DC 20.

Retrieve an opponent's weapon and deposit into your inventory when using Improved Disarm (Whip).
Range 15 feet, DC 20 if opponent is disarmed.

Use: Selected.
