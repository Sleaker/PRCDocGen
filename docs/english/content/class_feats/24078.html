:: Mystic Phoenix Stance ::

Type of Feat: Class Specific
Prerequisites: Jade Phoenix level 2
Specifics: While you use this ability, your caster level when you cast arcane spells increases by 1, and you gain a +2 dodge bonus to AC. If you expend an arcane spell, you gain damage reduction versus all but evil 2x the level of the spell expended (maximum damage reduction 10/evil for fifth level spell).
Use: Selected
