:: Hated Enemy Damage Reduction ::

Type of Feat: Class Specific
Prerequisite: Foe Hunter Level 2
Specifics: The Foe Hunter becomes increasingly immune to her hated foes attacks. At 2nd level she shrugs off 3 damage received per attack. The damage reduced increases by 2 ever other level to a maximum of 11/-. This damage resistance does not stack with any other damage resistance that the Foe Hunter may have.
Use: Automatic.
