:: Unseen Weapon: Unerring Strike ::

Feat Type: Class Feat
Prerequisite: Shadowblade 1
Description: As a standard action, you can wrap any melee weapon you wield in a layer of shifting shadows. Once per day per shadowblade level, you can activate one of the powers of your unseen weapon as a swift action. When you activate your unseen weapon, you must choose of the abilities. Multiple uses of the same ability do not stack on a single attack. At first level, you gain the ability to make an unerring strike.

Your next melee attack ignores any miss chance because of concealment or total concealment.
