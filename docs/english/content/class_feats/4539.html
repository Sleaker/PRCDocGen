:: Razor Storm ::

Type of Feat: Racial
Prerequisite: Race-Specific.
Specifics: Once per day, a bladeling can expel shrapnel-like bits of its skin in a 15' cone, dealing 2d6 points of slashing damage to any within the area. A reflex save vs 10 + the bladeling's Con modifier halves the damage. After this, the bladeling's natural armor bonus drops by 2 for 24 hours.
Use: Selected.
