:: Shadow Jump ::

Type of Feat: Class Specific
Prerequisite: Shadowlord level 1
Specifics: A Shadowlord can travel between shadows as a door dimension spell. He can jump up to a total 20 feet per class level per day (1 use == 10 feet). After a jump, he can't move until your next turn.
Use: Selected.

Feat Replaced By:

Shadow Jump
