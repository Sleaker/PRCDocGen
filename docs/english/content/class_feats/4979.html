:: Adept Fire Breath ::

Type of Feat: Breath Effect
Prerequisite: Dragonfire Adept level 1
You gain a breath weapon you can use at will as a standard action. Each time, you can choose between a 15' cone or a 30' line, with the distance doubling at level 10. It has a Reflex save DC of 10 + half your class level + your Con modifier for half damage, and does 1d6 of energy damage, increasing by 1d6 every 2 levels. You are immune to your own breath weapon, but not to other breath weapons of the same type.

You can breathe either a cone of fire or a line of fire.
Use: Selected
