:: Crusader Exorcism Of Steel ::

Iron Heart (Strike)
Level: Warblade 3
Prerequisite: One Iron Heart maneuver.
Initiation Action: 1 Standard Action
Range: Melee Attack
Target: One Creatures
Duration: 1 minute.
Save: Will partial, see text.

You attack, striking not your foe, but his weapon, sending a shockwave down his arm that leaves him unable to strike with full force.

You make a single melee attack. If it is successful, you attempt to damage the target's weapon. If you damage it successfully, it deals -4 damage. If the target succeeds on a will save, it deals -2 damage.
