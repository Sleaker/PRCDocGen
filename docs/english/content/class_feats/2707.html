:: Stunning Shout ::

Type of Feat: Class Specific
Prerequisite: Red Avenger Level 1
Specifics: The Red Avenger can release ki energy in a sonic attack. All enemies within the cone of effect are stunned for two full rounds unless they make a successful Fortitude save (DC 15 + Wisdom Modifier). Usable once per day per 4 levels of Red Avenger.
Use: Selected.

Feat Replaced By:

Stunning Shout
