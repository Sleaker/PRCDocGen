:: Anti-Paladin Extended Undetectable Alignment ::

School: Abjuration
Level: Brd 2, Clr 2, Pal 2
Components: V, S
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: Short
Target: One creature or object
Duration: 24 hours
Saving Throw: Will negates (object)
Spell Resistance: Yes (object)
Conceals the alignment of an object or a creature from the Detect Alignment series of spells
