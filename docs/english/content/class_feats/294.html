:: Turn Undead ::

Type of Feat: Class
Prerequisite: Cleric level 1, paladin level 3 or blackguard level 3.
Required for: Divine Might and Divine Shield.
Specifics: With this feat, the character can force undead to flee in terror. This ability may be activated three times per day, plus the cleric's Charisma modifier. The cleric's level and Charisma are used to determine how many undead are turned.
Use: Selected. If the cleric has twice as many levels as the undead have Hit Die, the undead are instantly destroyed.
