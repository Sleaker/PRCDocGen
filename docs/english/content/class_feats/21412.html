:: Wilder Telepathic Projection ::

Telepathy [Mind-Affecting]
Level: Psion/wilder 1
Manifesting Time: 1 standard action
Range: Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./ level)
Target: One creature
Duration: 1 min./level
Saving Throw: None
Power Resistance: No
Power Points: 1
Metapsionics: Extend

Using this power, you can grant a +4 bonus on your own (or others') Bluff, Intimidate, Perform, and Persuade checks.
