:: Draconic Wing Slams ::

At 12th level, a dragon disciple gains the ability to make 2 natural attacks with their wings, if of medium size or bigger. You can use your wing slams in addition to any weapons wielding in your hands.

Size - Damage
Medium - 1d3
Large - 1d4
Huge - 1d6
Gigantic - 1d8
Colossal - 2d6
