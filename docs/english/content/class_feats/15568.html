:: Archivist Implosion ::

Caster Level(s): Cleric 9
Innate Level: 9
School: Evocation
Descriptor(s):
Component(s): Verbal, Somatic
Range: Short
Area of Effect / Target: Medium
Duration: Instant
Additional Counter Spells:
Save: Fortitude Negates
Spell Resistance: Yes

The caster creates a vortex of destruction that tears asunder all living things within its area of effect, killing them instantly unless they make their saving throw.
