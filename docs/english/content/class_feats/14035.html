:: Archivist Quickened Snilloc's Snowball Swarm ::

Caster Level(s): Sorc/Wiz 2
Innate Level: 2
School: Evocation
Descriptor(s): Cold
Component(s): Verbal, Somatic
Range: Medium
Area of Effect / Target: Medium
Duration: Instant
Additional Counter Spells:
Save: Reflex half
Spell Resistance: Yes

A flurry of magic snowballs erupts in the target area. The swarm of snowballs deals 2d6 points of cold damage, plus 1d6 per 2 caster levels beyond third, to a maximum of 5d6 at level 9.
