:: Crusader Scorpion Parry ::

Setting Sun (Counter)
Level: Swordsage 6
Prerequisite: Two Setting Sun maneuvers
Initiation Action: 1 swift action
Range: Melee attack
Target: One creature

You knock your opponent's attack aside, guiding his weapon into one of his allies.

Make an opposed attack roll against the targeted creature. If you succeed, he damages an ally of his in melee range. If there is no enemy, he damages no one.
