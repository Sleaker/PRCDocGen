:: Swordsage Mirrored Pursuit ::

Setting Sun (Counter)
Level: Swordsage 5
Prerequisite: Two Setting Sun maneuvers
Initiation Action: 1 immediate action
Range: 30 feet
Target: One creature

Your movements perfectly match your foe's, allowing you to move as she does. Try as she might to escape, you remain at her side.

You immediately move to any spot adjacent to your enemy, provided it is within range.
