:: Healing Kicker (Reflex Saves) ::

At 3rd level, whenever you cast a healing spell on an ally, you can grant the target of your healing spell a competence bonus on Reflex saves equal to your class level. This effect lasts for 1 round.

You can use your Healing Kicker ability once per round, up to a total number of times per day equal to your class level + your Wisdom modifier (minimum once per day). Only one secondary effect can be imbued into any single spell, and it cannot be applied to yourself.
