:: Archivist Maximized Mass Aid ::

Enchantment (Compulsion) [Mind-Affecting]
Level: Clr 3
Range: Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
Targets: One or more creatures within a 30 ft. range.
Components: V, S, DF
Casting Time: 1 standard action
Duration: 1 min./level
Saving Throw: None
Spell Resistance: Yes (harmless)

Subjects gain +1 morale bonus on attack rolls and saves against fear effects, plus temporary hit points equal to 1d8 + caster level (to a maximum of 1d8 + 15 at caster level 15).
