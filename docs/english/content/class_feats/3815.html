:: Corellon's Wrath (Su) ::

Type of Feat: Class
Requirements: Champion of Corellon level 8
At 8th level, you gain the ability to briefly channel the divine power of Corellon Larethian, enveloping one melee weapon you wield in a veil of incandescent light as a free action. You gain a +2 sacred bonus on attack rolls and deal an extra 2d6 points of damage with each melee attack you make with the affected weapon for 1 round. While Corellon's Wrath is in effect, the weapon is considered magic and good-aligned for the purpose of overcoming damage reduction. You can use this ability a number of times a day equal to 3 + your Charisma modifier (minimum once per day)
