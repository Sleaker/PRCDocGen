:: Sorcerer Create Undead ::

Caster Level(s): Cleric 6, Wizard / Sorcerer 8
Innate Level:
School: Necromancy
Descriptor(s): Evil
Component(s): Verbal, Somatic
Range: Short
Area of Effect / Target: Point
Duration: 24 Hours
Additional Counter Spells: Sunbeam
Save: None
Spell Resistance: None

The caster brings an undead creature into being. The type of undead is based upon the caster's level:
Level 11 and under: Ghoul
Level 12-13: Ghast
Level 14-15: Wight
Level 16 and over: Spectre
