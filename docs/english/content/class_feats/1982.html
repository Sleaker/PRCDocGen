:: Ki Frenzy ::

Type of Feat: Class Specific
Prerequisite: Sohei level 1.
Specifics: You channel your Ki into a berserk frenzy, gaining +2 to Strength and Dexterity, 1 Extra attack per round, and a 33% movement boost. You take a -2 penalty to all attacks, and are unable to cast spells while in a frenzy. At the end of the frenzy, you are fatigued and take a -2 penalty to Strength and Constitution. Duration of both the Frenzy and the fatigue is 3 + Constitution rounds.
Use: Selected.

Feat Replaced By:

Ki Frenzy
