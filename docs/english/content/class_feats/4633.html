:: Treewalk ::

Type of Feat: Racial
Prerequisite: Race-Specific.
Specifics: Once per day, you can step into a nearby tree, and step out of any other one within 60'. You gain one extra use per day at level 5, and one more every 5 levels thereafter.
Note: For game purposes this is treated as a 60' Dimension Door ability that only works aboveground and in natural surroundings.
Use: Selected.
