:: Enervating Touch ::

Type of Feat: Class Specific
Prerequisite: Dread Necromancer 12.
Specifics: When a dread necromancer reaches 12th level, she gains the ability to bestow negative levels when she uses her charnel touch attack. Each day, she can use this ability a number of times equal to one-half her class level, granting a negative level with each touch. The saving throw against the negative levels has a DC of 10 + 1/2 her class level + her Charisma modifier.
Beginning at 17th level, the number of uses per day increases to equal her class level.
Use: Selected.
