:: Archivist Bloodfreeze Arrow ::

Bloodfreeze Arrow
Transmutation
Level: Assassin 4, ranger 4
Components: V, M
Casting Time: 1 swift action
Range: Long
Target: One creature
Duration: Instantaneous
Saving Throw: Fortitude partial; see text
Spell Resistance: Yes

When you cast this spell, you fire a magical or masterwork arrow or bolt at the target and transform the missile's head into blue ice. In addition to taking normal damage from the missile, the target takes 2d6 points of cold damage and is paralyzed. A successful Fortitude save negates the paralysis, and the target can make a new save each round (at the start of the caster's turn).

Material Component: Masterwork or magical arrow or bolt.
