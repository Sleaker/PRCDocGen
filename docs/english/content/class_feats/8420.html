:: Warblade Deathmark ::

Desert Wind (Strike) [Fire]
Level: Swordsage 3
Initiation Action: 1 Standard Action
Range: Melee Attack
Target: One Creature
Area: Variable.
Saving Throw: Reflex half, see text.

As your weapon strikes your foe, his body convulses as waves of flame run down your blade into his body. The fire causes him to briefly glow with a brilliant internal fire before the flames erupt from his body in a terrible explosion.

When you strike your foe, all creatures in the area (see table below) take 6d6 fire damage, Reflex save for half. You are immune to this effect.
This maneuver is a supernatural maneuver.
