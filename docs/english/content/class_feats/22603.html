:: Smite Good ::

Type of Feat: Template
Prerequisite: Fiendish Template, Half-Fiend Template.
Specifics: This feat allows the character to make a special attack against good creatures. Once per day a fiendish creature can make a normal melee attack to deal extra damage equal to its HD (maximum of +20) against an good foe.
Use: Selected. Smite Good only affects good creatures.
