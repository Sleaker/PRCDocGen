:: Dragonfire Adept Energy Immunity ::

Dark Invocation
8th Level Spell

You gain immunity to acid, cold, electricity, fire or energy, chosen when you cast the invocation. If you cast this invocation again before the duration ends, the new effect and duration replace the old. This invocation lasts for 24 hours.
