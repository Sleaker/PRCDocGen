:: Ranged Precision ::

Type of Feat: Class Specific
Prerequisite: Order of the Bow Initiate level 1.
Specifics: The Order of the Bow Initiate can perform a single attack on a creature no farther than 30 feet away. The attack has a damage bonus of 1d8. The bonus increase by 1d8 for every two Order of the Bow Initiate levels (2d8 at level 3, 3d8 at level 5, and so on). This does not work on creatures immune to critical hits.
Use: Selected.
