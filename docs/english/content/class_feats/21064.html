:: Psion Dissolving Weapon ::

Psychometabolism [Acid]
Level: Psychic warrior 2
Manifesting Time: 1 standard action
Range: Personal
Target: One held weapon; see text
Duration: Instantaneous
Saving Throw: None
Power Resistance: No
Power Points: 3
Metapsionics: Empower, Maximize, Twin

Your weapon is corrosive, and sizzling moisture visibly oozes from it. You deal 4d6 points of acid damage to any creature or object you touch with your successful melee attack. Your weapon is charged with acid until you make a successful attack.

Augment: For every 2 additional power points you spend, this power's damage increases by 1d6 points.
