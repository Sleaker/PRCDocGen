:: Effortless Healing (Ex) ::

Type of Feat: Class
Prerequisite: Level 7 Healer
Specifics: At 7th level, a healer has learned to cast spells of the healing subschool with minimal effort. She may cast such spells without provoking attacks of opportunity. This ability applies only to spells of the healing subschool that she cast as a healer, not to those that she may have by virtue of levels in another class.
Use: Automatic.
