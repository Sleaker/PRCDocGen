:: Dragonwrack (+4d6/2d6) ::

Type of Feat: Class Specific
Prerequisite: Vassal of Bahamut Level 4, 7 and 10.
Specifics: Evil dragons that strike a Vassal of Bahamut or are struck by him suffer grievous wounds. At 4th level the Vassal deals 2d6 damage if he strikes and 1d6 damage if he is struck by an evil creature of the dragon type. At 7th level this becomes 3d6 and 1d6. And at 10th it becomes 4d6 and 2d6.
Use: Automatic.
