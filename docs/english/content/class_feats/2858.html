:: Firewalk ::

Type of Feat: Class Specific
Prerequisite: Pyrokineticist level 6
Specifics: Beginning at 6th level, as a free action a pyrokineticist can expend her psionic focus (see the Concentration skill description, page 37) to move at twice her normal speed. She must pay 1 power point per round spent traveling in this fashion. Use the feat again to toggle the ability.
Use: Selected
