:: Bonus Domain: Dwarf ::

Type of Feat: Domain
Prerequisite: Access to Dwarf domain.
Granted Power: The cleric gains the Great Fortitude feat for free.
Bonus Spells: The cleric gains access to the following spells at the specified spell level: Glyph of Warding (2), Stoneskin (5), Protection from Spells (8)
Use: Automatic.
