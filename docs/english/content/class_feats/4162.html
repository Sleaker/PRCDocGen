:: Draconic Claws ::

At 2nd level, a dragon disciple gains 2 claws that can be used to make natural attacks with. You cannot use claws when wielding any weapons in your hands.

Size - Damage
Fine - 1d2
Diminutive - 1d2
Tiny - 1d2
Small - 1d3
Medium - 1d4
Large - 1d6
Huge - 1d8
Gigantic - 2d6
Colossal - 3d6
