:: Body of Flame ::

Type of Feat: Class Specific
Prerequisite: Disciple of Mephistopheles Level 10
Specifics: A Disciple of Mephistopheles can transform his body into flame for up to 10 minutes once per day. He gains fire immunity and damage reduction 30/+1, and anyone he touches or touches him must succeed a reflex save or catch fire.
Use: Selected.
