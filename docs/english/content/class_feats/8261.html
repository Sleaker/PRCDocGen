:: Swordsage Vanguard Strike ::

Devoted Spirit (Strike)
Level: Crusader 1
Initiation Action: 1 Standard Action
Range: Melee Attack
Target: One Creature

You batter aside your foe's defenses with a vicious, overwhelming attack, leaving him vulnerable to your allies blows.

You make a single attack against an enemy. If you hit, that enemy takes a -4 AC penalty for one round.
