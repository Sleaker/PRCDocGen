:: Censure Demons ::

Type of Feat: Class Specific
Prerequisite: Knight of the Chalice Level 1
Specifics: The Knight of the Chalice may attempt to censure demons, much like cleric turns undead. Any demon within 30 feet of the Knight must make a will save (DC + Knight of the Chalice Level + Cha Modifier) or be stunned for 1 round. If a censured demon has less than two times the Knight's class level HD, it must make a second will save or be destroyed.
Use: Selected.

Feat Replaced By:

Censure Demons
