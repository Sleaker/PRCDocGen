:: Summon Undead III ::

Type of Feat: Class Specific
Prerequisite: Master of Shrouds level 7
Duration: 1 round/ level
Specifics: A Master of Shrouds may summon a group of 4 undead a number of times per day equal to three plus her charisma modifier, with a minimum of one.
When not in combat, the summoned undead may turn on their summoner if they can overcome the summons Turn Undead ability.
Use: Selected.
