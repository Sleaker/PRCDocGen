:: Dragon Breath Weapon ::

You gain access to a Draconic Breath Weapon. The shape and element of this breath weapon varies based on what type of dragon disciple you are.

At level 3, this breath weapon will inflict 2d10 damage.
At level 7, it will inflict 4d10.
At level 10, it will inflict 6d10.
At level 13 and every three levels thereafter, your breath weapon will gain 2d10 damage.

The save DC of this breath attack is a reflex save of 10 + CON + 1/2 your Dragon Disciple level. Those passing this reflex save will take half damage.

Feat Replaced By:

Dragon Breath Weapon
