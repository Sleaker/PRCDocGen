:: Improved Healing ::

Type of Feat: Class
Prerequisite: Spellfire Channeler level 2.
Specifics: At 2nd level, the spellfire channeler can release stored spellfire energy levels to heal by touch. This restores 1d4+1 hit points per spellfire energy level expended (rather than the normal 2 points per spellfire energy level).
Use: Automatic.
