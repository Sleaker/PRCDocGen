Fist of Stone
Transmutation [Earth]
Level: Sorcerer/wizard 1, warmage 1
Components: V, S
Casting Time: 1 standard action
Range: Personal
Target: You
Duration: 1 minute

You transform one of your hands into a mighty fist of living stone, gaining a +6 enhancement bonus to Strength. In addition, you gain the ability to make one natural slam attack as a standard action, dealing 1d6 points of damage plus your new Strength bonus. You can make the slam attack as a natural secondary attack with the normal -5 penalty as part of a full attack action.

Your fist undergoes no change in size or form, remaining as flexible and responsive as it would normally be while under the spell's effect.
