:: Wilder Skate ::

Psychoportation
Level: Psion/wilder 1, psychic warrior 1
Manifesting Time: 1 standard action
Range: Personal or touch; see text
Target: You or one willing creature
Duration: 1 min./level
Saving Throw: None
Power Resistance: No
Power Points: 1
Metapsionics: Extend

You or another willing creature can slide along solid ground as if on smooth ice. If you manifest skate on yourself or another creature, the subject of the power retains equilibrium by mental desire alone, allowing her to gracefully skate along the ground, turn, or stop suddenly as desired. The skater's speed increases by 50%.
