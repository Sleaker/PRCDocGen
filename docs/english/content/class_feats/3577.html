:: Baelnorn Touch ::

Feat Type: Class Feat
Prerequisite: Baelnorn Level 1

Description: By touching an enemy, a Baelnorn may cause negative energy damage and possibly paralyze the target. The effects are as follows:

Level 1 - 1d6 + 5 negative energy damage, paralyzation 1d4 rounds.

Level 2 - 1d8 + 5 negative energy damage, paralyzation 1d4 minutes.

Level 3 - 1d8 + 5 negative energy damage, paralyzation 1d4 hours.

Level 4 - 1d8 + 5 negative energy damage, paralyzation permanent.


The character can make a Will save for half damage, and a Fortitude save against mind spells to avoid paralyzation (DC 10 + 1/2 Baelnorn's HD + CHA mod).
