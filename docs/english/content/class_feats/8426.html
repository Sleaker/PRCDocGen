:: Warblade Fire Riposte ::

Desert Wind (Counter)
Level: Swordsage 2
Initiation Action: 1 Immediate Action
Range: Personal.
Target: You.
Duration: Instantaneous.

You focus the pain from a wound you have just suffered into a fiery manifestation of revenge.

The next time you are struck during this round, you make a melee touch attack. If it succeeds, the target takes 4d6 fire damage.
This is a supernatural maneuver.
