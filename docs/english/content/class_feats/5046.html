:: Epic Spell: Battle Bounding ::

School: Transmutation [Teleportation]
Components: V,S
Range: Personal
Area: 10-ft.-radius aura
Duration: 20 rounds
Saving Throw: None
Spell Resistance: None

Spell casters do not suffer interruptions gladly, and the creation of this spell was a result of that. When an enemy enters the aura, created by this spell, the caster is instantly teleported to a different location nearby. In essence, the spell is designed to keep the caster in the battle while keeping the battle out of arms reach of the caster.
