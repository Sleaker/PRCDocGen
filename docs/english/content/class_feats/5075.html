:: Researched Epic Spell: Epic Repulsion ::

School: Abjuration
Components: V,S
Range: Touch
Target: Creature or object touched
Duration: 24 hours
Saving Throw: None
Spell Resistance: Yes, see text

You can create a ward against a specific type of creature for the duration. Any creature of the specific type cannot come within the aura of the warded creature or object. Spell resistance can allow a creature to overcome this protection and enter the aura of the warded subject.
