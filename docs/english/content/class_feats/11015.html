:: Ocular Adept Tongue of Baalzebul ::

Tongue of Baalzebul
Transmutation [Evil]
Level: Clr 1
Components: V, S, M, Drug
Casting Time: 1 full round
Range: Personal
Target: Caster
Duration: 1 hour/level

The caster gains the ability to lie, seduce and beguile with devil's skill. He gains a +2 competence bonus on Bluff, Diplomacy, and Gather information checks.

Material Component: A tongue from any creature capable of speech.

Drug Component: Mushroom powder.
