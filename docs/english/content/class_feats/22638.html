:: Lich touch ::

Type of Feat: Class Specific
Prerequisite: Lich Level 1
Specifics: This touch attack only effects the living. When hit, your foe must make a fortitude save vs. 10 + half your level + Charisma modifier or be paralyzed as well.
Use: Selected.

2: 1d8+5

4: 1d8+5
5: 4d6+5
6: 6d6+10
7: 8d6+15
8: 10d6+20
