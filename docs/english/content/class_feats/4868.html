:: Expanded Knowledge II ::

Type of Feat: Psionic
Prerequisites: Manifester level 3rd
Specifics: Add to your powers known one additional power of any level up to one level lower than the highest-level power you can manifest. For example, a 7th-level psion gains either a new 1st, 2nd, or 3rd level power. You can choose any power, including powers from another discipline's list or even from another class' list.
Special: You can take this feat multiple times. Each time, you learn one new power at any level up to one less than the highest-level power you can manifest.
Use: Automatic

Requires All of These Feats:

Expanded Knowledge I

Feat Replaced By:

Expanded Knowledge III

Required For the Following Feats:

Expanded Knowledge III
