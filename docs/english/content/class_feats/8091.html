:: Crusader Scything Blade ::

Iron Heat (Boost)
Level: Warblade 7
Prerequisite Three Iron Heart maneuvers
Initiation Action: 1 swift action
Range: Personal
Target: You

You strike at one foe with a long, high backhand cut, then make a quick turn to continue the stroke against another nearby enemy.

If the first melee attack you make during your turn hits, you can immediately make a free attack at your highest attack bonus against a different enemy that you threaten. You can only gain one free attack each time you initiate this maneuver, regardless of how many successful attacks you make in this round.
