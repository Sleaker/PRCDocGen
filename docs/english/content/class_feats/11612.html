:: Duskblade Ghoul Touch ::

Caster Level(s): Wizard / Sorcerer 2
Innate Level: 2
School: Necromancy
Descriptor(s): Negative
Component(s): Verbal, Somatic
Range: Touch
Area of Effect / Target: Single
Duration: 1d6 + 2 Rounds
Additional Counter Spells: Cure Light Wounds
Save: Fortitude Negates
Spell Resistance: Yes

The caster's hand glows with an unearthly radiance. The target creature must make a Fortitude save or become paralyzed. Creatures paralyzed by this spell emit a noxious cloud that applies a -2 penalty to skills, saving throws, attack and damage rolls to all creatures within 5 feet.
