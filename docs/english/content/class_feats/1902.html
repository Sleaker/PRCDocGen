:: Swashbuckler Grace 3 ::

Type of Feat: Class Specific
Prerequisite: Swashbuckler 20
Specifics: gains a +3 bonus on Reflex saves at 20th level. This bonus increases every 9 levels. A swashbuckler loses this bonus when wearing medium or heavy armor, or while encumbered.
Use: Automatic.

Feat Replaced By:

Swashbuckler Grace 4
