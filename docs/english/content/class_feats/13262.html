:: Mystic Soul Scour ::

Caster Level(s): Cleric 5
Innate Level: 5
School: Necromancy
Descriptor(s):
Component(s): Verbal, Somatic
Range: Touch
Area of Effect / Target: Living creature touched
Duration: Instant
Additional Counter Spells:
Save: Will negates
Spell Resistance: Yes

This spell deal 2d6 points of charisma damage and 1d6 points of wisdom damage. It then deals an additional 1d6 charisma damage 1 minute later.
