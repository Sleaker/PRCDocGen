:: Swordsage War Leader's Charge ::

White Raven (Strike)
Level: Crusader 6, Warblade 6
Prerequisite: Two White Raven maneuvers
Initiation Action: 1 Full-round Action
Range: Melee Attack
Target: One Creature

You summon a great fury within your lungs, releasing it in a titantic shout as you charge forward. Your reckless move startles your foes and puts greater force behind your attack.

You charge your opponent, dealing an extra 35 damage if you hit. You take no AoOs from the movement.
