:: Epic Spell: Unimpinged ::

School: Abjuration
Components: V,S
Range: Touch
Target: Creature touched
Duration: Caster Level + 10 rounds.
Saving Throw: None (Harmless)
Spell Resistance: No (Harmless)

The subject of a casting of Unimpinged becomes impervious to half of all bludgeoning damage, effectively negating any physical attacks from clubs, maces, flails, and the like. (50% Immunity is given)
