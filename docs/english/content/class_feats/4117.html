:: Dragon Blood, Obscure - Chaos ::

The blood of a Chaos Dragon courses through your veins. In addition to standard Dragon Disciple progression, you receive certain specific benefits befitting your Chaos Dragon ancestry.

At level 10 in the Dragon Disciple class, you receive total immunity to 'confusion' effects.

At level 3, you gain the ability to breath a 60 foot line of completely random energy once per day. This line will cause 2d10 damage, increasing to 4d10 at level 7, 6d10 at level 10, with further improvements in epic levels.
