:: Wild Frenzy ::

You can spend one of your turn/rebuke undead attempts as a swift action to gain a +2 bonus on attack rolls and damage rolls with weapons and eldritch blasts, along with temporary hit points equal to twice your class level. This effect lasts for a number of rounds equal to 3 + your Cha modifier (minimum 1 round).
Your deity must be chaotic for you to select this gift.
