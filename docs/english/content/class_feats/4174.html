:: Magical Melody ::

Type of Feat: Class
Prerequisite: Virtuoso Level 8, Perform 18 ranks.
Specifics: A 8th-level virtuoso with at least 18 ranks in Perform can empower allied spellcasters, raising their effective caster levels by +1 for the purposes of spell effects and spell resistance checks. Magical melody requires two daily uses of virtuoso performance. It is a supernatural ability.
Use: Selected.
