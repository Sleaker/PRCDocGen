Tiger Claw (Stance)
Level: Swordsage 8, Warblade 8
Prerequisite: Two Tiger Claw maneuvers
Initiation Action: 1 Swift Action
Range: Personal
Target: You
Duration: Stance

With each stinging attach that connects against a foe, you slip around him, using the distraction provided by your attacks to prevent him from hindering your movement.

While you are in this stance, you gain a 5 foot bonus to movement for every successful attack, up to a maximum of 30 feet. This bonus applies the round after the hits strike.
