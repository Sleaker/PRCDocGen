:: Drain Charged Item ::

Type of Feat: Class
Prerequisite: Spellfire Channeler level 1.
Specifics: As a standard action, a spellfire channeler can drain charges from a touched charged item (including single-use items such as potions and scrolls), converting the energy into stored spellfire energy levels. A creature gets to make a Will save (DC 10) to prevent one of its held or carried items from being drained.
Use: Selected.
