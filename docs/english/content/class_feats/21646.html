:: Fist of Zuoken Painful Strike ::

Psychometabolism
Level: Psychic warrior 2
Manifesting Time: 1 standard action
Range: Personal
Target: You
Duration: 1 round/level
Power Points: 3
Metapsionics: Extend

Your natural weapons cause additional pain. Each successful attack you make with a natural weapon deals an extra 1d4 points of bludgeoning damage to the target.
