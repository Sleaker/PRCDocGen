:: Ancestral Daisho ::

Type of Feat: Class
Prerequisite: Level 4 Samurai

Specifics: All Samurai carry two masterwork weapons - a katana and a wakizashi. These weapons have been handed down over generations from the Samurai's ancestors, and protecting them is an important point of honor for the Samurai. As a Samurai acquires wealth, he can awaken the supernatural abilities latent in the weapons by sacrificing items to enhance them.

Use: Selected.
