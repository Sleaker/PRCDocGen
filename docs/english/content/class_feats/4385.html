:: Epic Order of the Bow Initiate ::

Your mastery of the bow is legend. You can fell foes with a single strike, and the entire Order of the Bow looks up to your mastery. You are the supreme wielder of the bow.

Hit Die: d8
Skill Points at Each Additional Level: 2+ Int Modifier
Bonus Feats: The epic Order of the Bow Initiate gains a bonus feat every three levels after 10th
Ranged Precision: Your ranged precision damage increases to 6d8 at level 11, and continues to increase at the same rate (7d8 at 13, 8d8 at 15, and so on).

Epic Order of the Bow Initiate Bonus Feats List:
Blinding Speed, Epic Prowess, Weapon Focus: Long/Short Bow, Overwhelming Critical: Long/Short Bow, Devastating Critical: Long/Short Bow, Manyshot, Improved Manyshot, Pinpoint Accuracy, Great Dexterity, Epic Reflexes, Epic Toughness.
