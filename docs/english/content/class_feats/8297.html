:: Swordsage Mithral Tornado ::

Iron Heart (Strike)
Level: Warblade 4
Prerequisite: Two Iron Heart Maneuvers
Initiation Action: 1 Standard action
Range: Melee Attack
Target: Adjacent Creatures

Your weapon becomes a blur of motion as you swing it in a tight arc over your head. Once you build up enough speed, you explode into a sweeping attack that chops the enemies around you.

You make a single attack against each adjacent opponent with a +2 bonus.
