:: Psion Ectoplasmic Sheen ::

Metacreativity (Creation)
Level: Psion/wilder 1
Manifesting Time: 1 standard action
Range: Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
Target or Area: 10-ft. square
Duration: 1 round/level
Saving Throw: See text
Power Resistance: No
Power Points: 1
Metapsionics: Extend

You create a pool of ectoplasm across the floor that inhibits motion and can cause people to slow down. This functions as the spell grease.
