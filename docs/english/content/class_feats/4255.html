:: Frightful Presence ::

Type of Feat: Class
Prerequisite: Level 3 Fist of Hextor
Specifics: When a Fist of Hextor reaches 3rd level, he gains the extraordinary ability to instill fear in others once per day. All those (except for other Fists) who fail the save are frightened for 5d6 rounds. Those who succeed are merely shaken (Doom effect). The DC is 10 + Fist level + Cha modifier. The Fist gains an extra use of this ability at levels 6, 9, 13, 16, 19, 23, 26, 29.
Use: Selected

Feat Replaced By:

Frightful Presence
