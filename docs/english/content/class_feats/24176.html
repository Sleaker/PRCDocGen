:: Blessing of Dawn ::

Type of Feat: Class.
Prerequisite: Morninglord of Lathander level 6.
Specifics: The sight of the morning sun is an inspirational vision for all morninglords. Beginning at 6th level, a Morninglord of Lathander gains a +2 morale bonus on Will saves from sunrise until noon. This ability is only in effect while she can see the sun; the effect is suppressed any time she is deprived the sight of it during this period.
