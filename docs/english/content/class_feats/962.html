:: Dragon Abilities ::

Type of Feat: Class
Prerequisite: Red Dragon Disciple.
Specifics: As the dragon disciple gains levels in this prestige class, his ability scores increase by the following table:
Level
2 : +2 to Strength
4 : +2 to Strength
7 : +2 to Con
9 : +2 to Int
10: +4 to Strength, +2 to Charisma
Use: Automatic
