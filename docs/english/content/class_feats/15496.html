:: Archivist Silent Greater Planar Ally ::

Caster Level(s): Cleric 8
Innate Level: 8
School: Conjuration (Calling)
Descriptor(s): [see text]
Component(s): V, S, DF
Range: Short
Area of Effect / Target: Point
Duration: 1 hour / level
Save: None
Spell Resistance: No

An Outsider is summoned to assist the caster. The type of Outsider varies with the caster's alignment:
Evil: Vrock
Neutral: Death slaad
Good: Celestial Avenger

Note: When you use a calling spell that calls an air, chaotic, earth, evil, fire, good, lawful, or water creature, it is a spell of that type.
