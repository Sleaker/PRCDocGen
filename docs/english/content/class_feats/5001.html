:: Epic Spell Seed: Animate ::

Transmutation

This is a spell seed, and is required to successfully research any Epic Spells relating to this seed.

You can imbue inanimate objects with mobility and a semblance of life (not actual life). The animated object can be of any nonmagical material: wood, metal, stone, fabric, leather, ceramic, glass, and so forth. You can also animate part of a larger mass of raw matter, such as a volume of water in the ocean, part of a stony wall, or perhaps even part of the earth itself (though, there are limits of course).
