:: Daunting Challenge ::

Type of Feat: Class Specific
Prerequisite: Knight 12.
Specifics: As a swift action, you shout a challenge against all opponents within 100'. Foes must have a Challenge Rating equal to at least your level minus two, and an Intelligence of 5 or greater to be affected. If they fail a Will save vs 10 + 1/2 class level + Charisma modifier, your enemies are shaken.
This costs one use of your Knight's Challenge.
Use: Selected.
