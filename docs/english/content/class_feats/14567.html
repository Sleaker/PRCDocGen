:: Archivist Extended Armor of Darkness ::

Caster Level(s): Darkness 4
Innate Level: 4
School: Abjuration
Descriptor(s): Darkness
Component(s): Verbal, Somatic
Range: Touch
Area of Effect / Target: Single
Duration: 10 Turn /lvl
Additional Counter Spells:
Save: Will negate (harmless)
Spell Resistance: Yes (harmless)
The target have a + 3 deflection bonus to Armor Class and an additional +1 for every 4 caster levels (max +8), gains a +2 saving throw bonus against holy and good. Undead creatures gain also +4 turn resistance.
