:: Holy Blade ::

Type of Feat: Class
Prerequisite: Level 5 Shining Blade
Specifics: Adds damage bonuses of 1d6 Electrical and 2d6 Divine vs. Evil to your weapon. Duration is a number of rounds equal to your class level + charisma modifier. Can be used 4/day at level 5, and 5/day at level 7.
Use: Selected.

Feat Replaced By:

Brilliant Blade
