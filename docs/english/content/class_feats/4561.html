:: Minor Change Shape - General ::

Type of Feat: Racial
Prerequisite: Race Specific
You can physically alter your form to mimic that of another of similar size and mass. You can appear one foot shorter or taller, fatter or thinner, or even change genders, but you cannot grow new limbs.
This feat controls learning and using NPC shapes. Return to True Form is under this feat as well.
Use: Selected.
