:: Channel Spell 2 ::

Type of Feat: Class Specific
Prerequisite: Level 4 or higher in the Spellsword prestige class.
Specifics: The Spellsword can channel any spell he can cast into his melee weapon.
Using this ability requires the spellsword to cast the spells to his equipped melee
weapon. The Channeled spell affects the next target that the spellsword successfully attacks.
Even if the spell normally affects an area or is a ray, it affects only the target.
Level
4: Channel Spell 3 times per day.
6: Channel Spell 4 times per day.
8: Channel Spell 5 times per day.

Epic Levels:
14: Channel Spell 6 times per day.
16: Channel Spell 7 times per day.
18: Channel Spell 8 times per day.
24: Channel Spell 9 times per day.
26: Channel Spell 10 times per day.
