:: Warblade Charging Minotaur ::

Stone Dragon (Strike)
Level: Crusader 1, Swordsage 1, Warblade 1
Initiation Action: 1 Full-Round Action
Range: Melee Attack
Target: One Creature

You charge at your foe, blasting him with such power that he stumbles back.

Make a Bull Rush attack as part of a charge. You take no AoOs for this action. If you succeed on the strength check, you deal 2d6 + Str bludgeoning damage, and the target is pushed back 5 feet, and possibly more.
