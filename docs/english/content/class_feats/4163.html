:: Draconic Bite and Claws ::

At 2nd level, a dragon disciple gains a bite that can be used to make a natural attack with. You can use your bite in addition to any weapons wielding in your hands.

At 2nd level, a dragon disciple also gains 2 claw attacks that can be used to make a natural attack with. You cannot use your claws in addition to any weapons wielding in your hands.

Size - Damage (bite/claw)
Fine - 1d2/1d2
Diminutive - 1d2/1d2
Tiny - 1d3/1d2
Small - 1d4/1d3
Medium - 1d6/1d4
Large - 1d8/1d6
Huge - 2d6/1d8
Gigantic - 3d6/2d6
Colossal - 4d6/3d6
