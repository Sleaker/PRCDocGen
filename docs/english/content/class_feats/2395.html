:: Epic Ravager ::

The Epic Ravager has embraced the depths of madness. They are engines of pure destructive rage, treachery, and focused hate.

An Epic Ravager gains powers in the following order every 5 levels.

Pain Touch
Aura of Fear
Cruelest Cut
Bonus Feat
Visage of Terror

Bonus Feat List
Epic Skill Focus(Intimidate), Epic Skill Focus(Move Silently), Epic Weapon Focus, Overwhelming Critical, Devastating Critical, Great Strength, Great Dexterity, Great Charisma, Epic Toughness, Epic Prowess, Armour Skin, Epic DR.
