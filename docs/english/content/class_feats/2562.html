:: Elemental Perfection ::

Type of Feat: Class Specific
Prerequisite: Elemental Savant level 10
Specifics: You gain all the benefits of being an elemental, including immunity to poison, sleep, paralysis, stunning, critical hits and sneak attacks. You also gain the special attacks and special qualities of a medium elemental of the chosen type. You become immune to the chosen element, but take double damage from all spells of the opposing element.
Use: Automatic.
