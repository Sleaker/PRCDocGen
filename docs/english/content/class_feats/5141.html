:: Researched Epic Spell: Risen Reunited ::

School: Conjuration (Healing)
Components: V,S
Range: Personal
Target: Any known deceased creature
Duration: Instantaneous
Saving Throw: None (harmless)
Spell Resistance: No (harmless)

You concentrate on any deceased creature*, regardless of proximity, and resurrect them. Following the instant they regain life, the spell teleports the subject to your location.

*Upon casting this spell, a conversation will appear listing all available players who are dead. Choose the player from this list to resurrect and teleport to you.
