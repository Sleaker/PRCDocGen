:: Jump ::

Ability: Strength.
Requires Training: no.
Classes: All.

A successful check allows a character to jump over pits, vault low fences. Halfling have a +2 racial bonus.

Synergy: If you have 5+ rank in Tumble, you get a +2 to Jump checks. If you have 5+ ranks in Jump you get +2 to Tumble checks.

Use: Automatic.
