:: Bard Disguise Self ::

School: Illusion (Glamer)
Level: Bard/Sorcerer/Wizard/Hexblade 1
Components: V, S
Range: Personal
Target: Self
Duration: 10 min/level
Save: Will
Spell Resistance: No

You make yourself - including clothing, armor, weapons and equipment - look different. There is no actual change to your abilities or equipment. A creature that interacts with the glamer gets a will save (DC 10 + your CHA mod) to recognize the illusion.
