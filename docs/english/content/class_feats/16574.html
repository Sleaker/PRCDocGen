:: Favoured Soul Crafter's Blessing ::

Transmutation
Level: Clr 1, Wit 1
Components: V, S, M/DF
Casting Time: 1 minute
Range: Touch
Target: Creature touched
Duration: 1 week
Saving Throw: Will negates (harmless)
Spell Resistance: Yes (harmless)

The creature you touch gains good fortune and skill in crafts, gaining a +10 competence bonus on Craft checks for a week's worth of work. The subject's work will generally be faster and of better quality than normal. It will at least be competent, although an unskilled crafter will still not easily produce masterworks.

Arcane material component: A few wood shavings and a small piece of metal.
