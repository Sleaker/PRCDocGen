:: Crusader Time Stands Still ::

Diamond Mind (Strike)
Level: Swordsage 9, warblade 9
Prerequisite: Four Diamond Mind maneuvers
Initiation Action: 1 full-round action
Range: Personal
Target: You

The raindrops themselves stand still as you act at the speed of thought. You move like a blur, catching your enemies by surprise with a complex action carried our in a tiny fraction of the time normally needed to complete it.

You take your full attack action twice against a single foe.
