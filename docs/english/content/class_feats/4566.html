:: Shifting ::

Type of Feat: Racial
Prerequisite: Shifter
You can "shift" and assume aspects of your ancestral heritage. You gain a +2 bonus to a specific stat and one other bonus when shifting. Shifting lasts for 3 rounds, plus the shifter's Con modifier. plus one round for each shifter feat they have. They can use this ability once per day, and for every two shifter feats they have they gain one additional use per day.
Use: Selected.

Required For the Following Feats:

Extra Shifter Trait
Healing Factor
Shifter Defense
Shifter Ferocity
Shifter Instincts
