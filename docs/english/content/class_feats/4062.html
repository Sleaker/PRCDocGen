:: Tyranny ::

Type of Feat: Domain
Prerequisite: Access to Tyranny domain.
Granted Power: The cleric gains +2 to the DC of any mind spell he casts.
Bonus Spells: The cleric gains access to the following spells at the specified spell level: Bestow Curse (2), Fear (3), Bigby's Grasping Hand (7), Dominate Monster (9)
Use: Automatic.
