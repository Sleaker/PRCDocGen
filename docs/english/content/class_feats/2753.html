:: Epic Red Avenger ::

Your mastery of ki energy is unmatched. By harnessing its awesome power, you can perform feats that most can only dream of.

Hit Die: d8
Skill Points at Each Additional Level: 4+ Int Modifier
Bonus Feats: The epic Red Avenger gains a bonus feat every four levels.
Special:
Ki Powers: Gain additional uses per day every four levels.
Free Ki: Increases to 3x at level 20 and 4x at level 30

Epic Red Avenger Bonus Feats List:
Armor Skin, Epic Damage Reduction, Epic Toughness, Great Wisdom, Epic Energy Resistance, Blinding Speed, Self Concealment
