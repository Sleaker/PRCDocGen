:: Swordsage Salamander Charge ::

Desert Wind (Strike) [Fire]
Level: Swordsage 7
Prerequisite: Three Desert Wind maneuvers
Initiation Action: 1 Full-round action
Range: Personal
Target: You
Duration: Instantaneous, see text

You spin and tumble about the battlefield, a wall of raging flame marking your steps.

You charge your foe. In the space across which you charge, a wall of fire appears, dealing 6d6 damage to all who enter. This wall lasts for 5 rounds.
This is a supernatural maneuver.
