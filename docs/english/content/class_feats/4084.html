:: Crack of Doom ::

Type of Feat: Class
Prerequisite: Lasher level 8.
Specifics: At 8th level, a lasher can take two extra attacks per round with a whip. This ability supersedes crack of fate (the abilities do not stack). The attack is at the lasher's highest base attack bonus, but each attack (the extra ones and the normal ones) suffers a -4 penalty.
Use: Selected.
