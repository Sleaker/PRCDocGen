Clairsentience
Level: Psion/wilder 4
Display: Mental and visual
Manifesting Time: 1 standard action
Range: Personal
Target: You
Duration: 24 hours
Saving Throw: None
Power Resistance: No
Power Points: 7
Metapsionics: Extend.

You immediately become aware of any attempt to observe you by means of a clairsentience (scrying) power or divination (scrying) spell. You and the remote viewer immediately make opposed manifester level checks. If you succeed you learn the manifester's name and location.
