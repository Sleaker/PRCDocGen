:: Fear Aura ::

Type of Feat: Class.
Prerequisite: Level 9 Warpriest.

Specifics: Twice per day, the Warpriest can project an aura of fear to a radius of 20 feet. The effect lasts for 1 round per Warpriest level. Foes within the area of effect must make a Will save (DC 10 + Warpriest level + Cha bonus) or be affected as if by a fear spell.

Use: Selected.
