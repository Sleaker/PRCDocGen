:: Fang Half-Dragon ::

The blood of a Fang Dragon courses through your veins. In addition to standard Half-Dragon properties, you receive certain specific benefits befitting your Fang Dragon ancestry.

As a Fang Half-Dragon, you do not gain any immunities.

You do not gain a breath weapon as standard Half-Dragons do. Rather you gain the ability to perform a special bite attack that will deal 1d4 points of Constitution Drain to your target. You may use this special bite attack 3 times a day.
