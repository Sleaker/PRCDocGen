:: Ki Power ::

Type of Feat: Class Specific
Prerequisite: Ninja Level 1
Specifics: A ninja can channel her ki energy to manifest special powers of stealth and mobility. She can use her ki powers a number of times per day equal to one half her class level (minimum 1) plus her Wisdom bonus (if any). Ki powers can be used only if a ninja is wearing no armor and is unencumbered.
As long as a ninja's ki pool isn't empty (that is, as long as she has at least one daily use remaining), she gains a +2 bonus to her Will saves.
Use: Automatic.

Required For the Following Feats:

Ascetic Stalker
Expanded Ki Pool
Ghost Step
Ghost Step (ethereal)
Ghost Strike
Ghost Walk
Greater Ki Dodge
Ki Dodge
