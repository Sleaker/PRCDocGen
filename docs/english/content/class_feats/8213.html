:: Swordsage Desert Tempest ::

Desert Wind (Strike)
Level: Swordsage 6
Prerequisite: Two Desert Wind maneuvers
Initiation Action: 1 Full-round Action
Range: Personal
Target: You
Duration: 1 round.

You move in a blur, leaving scorch marks in your wake as you twirl around the battlefield, slicing into your foes as you move.

Move up to your speed. Each time you move next to an enemy, make a single melee attack against him.
No enemy can be struck more than once.
