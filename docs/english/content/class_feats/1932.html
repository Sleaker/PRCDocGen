:: Major Aura - Resilient Troops ::

Type of Feat: Class Specific
Prerequisite: Marshal
Specifics: While this Major Aura is active, you and all allies within a 60 foot radius receive a bonus on all saving throws. This does not stack with minor Auras. This bonus is based on your level. It is a +1 bonus at level 2, gaining an additional +1 at levels 7, 14, 20, 25, 30, 35, and 40.
Use: Activated. A Marshal can only have 1 Minor and 1 Major aura active at any given time.
