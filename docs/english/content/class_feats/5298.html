:: Cold ::

Type of Feat: Domain
Prerequisite: Access to the Cold domain.
Granted Power: You can turn or destroy fire creatures as a good cleric turns undead. You can also rebuke or command cold creatures as an evil cleric rebukes undead.
Bonus Spells: The cleric gains access to the following spells at the specified spell level: Chill Touch (1), Ice Storm (4), Cone of Cold (6), and Polar Ray (8)
Use: Automatic.
