:: Crusader Rising Phoenix ::

Desert Wind (Stance) [Fire]
Level: Swordsage 8
Prerequisite: Three Desert Wind maneuvers
Initiation Action: 1 Swift Action
Range: Personal
Target: You
Duration: Stance

Hot winds swirl about your feet, lifting you skyward as flames begin to flick below.

You gain freedom of movement. You may attack an adjacent enemy with an extra 3d6 fire damage. (Use the feat added).
This is a supernatural maneuver.
