:: Bard Weapon of Impact ::

Caster Level(s): Wizard / Sorcerer 3, Bard 3
Innate Level: 3
School: Transmutation
Descriptor(s): Weapon Enchantment
Component(s): Verbal, Somatic
Range: Short
Area of Effect / Target: Creature or Bludgeoning Weapon
Duration: 10 Minutes / Level
Additional Counter Spells:
Save: None
Spell Resistance: No

Adds the Keen property to the targeted bludgeoning melee weapon, increasing its critical threat range.
