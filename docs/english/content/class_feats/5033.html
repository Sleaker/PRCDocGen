:: Researched Epic Spell: All Hope Lost ::

School: Enchantment [Mind-Affecting]
Components: V, S
Range: Personal
Target: Self
Area: 40' Radius
Duration: 20 rounds
Saving Throw: Will negates
Spell Resistance: Yes

With the casting of this spell, you force overwhelming feelings of despair on all affected creatures within your immediate area. On a failed saving throw, the emotion persists for the duration.
