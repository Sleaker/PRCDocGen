:: Crusader Mighty Throw ::

Setting Sun (Strike)
Level: Swordsage 1
Initiation Action: 1 Standard Action
Range: Melee Attack.
Target: One Creature.

You use superior leverage and your Setting Sun training to send an opponent tumbling to the ground.

Make a trip attempt against your target. You get a +4 bonus on this attempt and cannot be tripped if you fail on this attempt. If you succeed on the check, your enemy is thrown 10 feet away and lands prone on the ground.
