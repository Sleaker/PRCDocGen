:: Favoured Soul Control Weather ::

Caster Level(s): Air 7, Clr 7, Drd 7, Sor/Wiz 7
Innate Level: 7
School: Transmutation
Casting Time: 10 minutes; see text
Component(s): Verbal, Somatic
Range: Range: The current area
Area: The current area; see text
Duration: 4d12 hours; see text
Additional Counter Spells:
Saving Throw: None
Spell Resistance: No

You change the weather in the local area. It takes 10 minutes to cast the spell and then the effects manifest. You can call forth various weather, including changing it to Snow, Rain, or making the weather clear.

Apart from the few spells that rely upon certain weather conditions, this spell provides purely visual effects.

A druid casting this spell doubles the duration.
