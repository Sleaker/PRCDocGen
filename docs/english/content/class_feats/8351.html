:: Swordsage Ancient Mountain Hammer ::

Stone Dragon (Strike)
Level: Crusader 7, Swordsage 7, Warblade 7
Prerequisite: Three Stone Dragon maneuvers
Initiation Action: 1 Standard Action
Range: Melee Attack
Target: One Creature

You put the weight of a great mountain behind your attack, pounding through armor and bone.

As part of this maneuver, you make a single melee attack. This attack deals an extra 12d6 points of damage and automatically overcomes damage reduction.
