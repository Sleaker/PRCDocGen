:: Dragonfire Adept Instill Vulnerability ::

Dark Invocation
7th Level Spell

You imbue a single creature within 30' with 100% vulnerability to acid, cold, electricity, fire or energy, chosen when you cast the invocation. If you cast this invocation again before the duration ends, the new effect and duration replace the old. This invocation lasts for 24 hours.
