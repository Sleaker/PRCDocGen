:: Crusader Fountain Of Blood ::

Tiger Claw (Boost)
Level: Swordsage 4, Warblade 4
Prerequisite: Two Tiger Claw maneuvers
Initiation Action: 1 Swift Action
Range: 30 ft.
Area: 30 ft burst.
Duration: 1 minute
Saving Throw: Will partial; see text

With a war cry, you leap into the air and lift your weapon high overhead. As you arc downward, your weight and momentum lend bone-crushing force to your attack.

You strike a foe with less than 10 hit points for an additional 1d6, causing all creatures within the area to make a will save or be shaken for one minute.
