:: Sustaining Song ::

Type of Feat: Class
Prerequisite: Virtuoso Level 1, Perform 11 ranks.
Specifics: A 1st-level virtuoso with at least 11 ranks in Perform can sustain her unconscious allies, negating their need for stabilization checks during her performance. Because they're not making stabilization checks, the affected allies are neither stabilizing nor losing hit points. Sustaining song is a supernatural ability.
Use: Selected.
