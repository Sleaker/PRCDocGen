:: Thousand Faces - Medium ::

Type of Feat: Class.
Prerequisite: Level 7 Ninja Spy.

Specifics: Using masterful skills of disguise, a Ninja Spy can mask their identity, even altering their appearance to resemble a member of a different race. Use of this skill allows the Ninja Spy to take on the appearance of an Elf, Dwarf, Half-orc, Half-elf or Human. The change is purely cosmetic and no racial feats or bonuses from the race imitated are bestowed through use of this skill.

Use: Selected.
