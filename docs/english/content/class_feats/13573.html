:: Archivist Extended Hide From Undead ::

Abjuration
Level: Clr 1, Dn 1
Components: V, S, DF
Casting Time: 1 standard action
Range: Touch
Targets: One creature touched
Duration: 10 min./level (D)
Saving Throw: Will negates (harmless)
Spell Resistance: Yes

Undead cannot see, hear, or smell the warded creatures. Even extraordinary or supernatural sensory capabilities, such as blindsense, blindsight, scent, and tremorsense, cannot detect or locate warded creatures. If a warded creature attempts to turn or command undead, touches an undead creature, or attacks any creature (even with a spell), the spell ends for all recipients.
