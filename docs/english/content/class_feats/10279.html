:: Sorcerer Tasha's Hideous Laughter ::

Caster Level(s): Bard 2, Wizard / Sorcerer 2
Innate Level: 2
School: Enchantment
Descriptor(s):
Component(s): Verbal, Somatic
Range: Short
Area of Effect / Target: Single
Duration: 1d3 rounds
Additional Counter Spells:
Save: Will negates
Spell Resistance: Yes

If the target fails their saving throw they will begin laughing hysterically. They will be unable to defend themselves until the spell wears off. A creature whose racial type is different from the caster's gains a +4 bonus on its saving throw because humor doesn't 'translate' well.
