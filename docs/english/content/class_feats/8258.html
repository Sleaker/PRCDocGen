:: Swordsage Strike Of Righteous Vitality ::

Devoted Spirit (Strike)
Level: Crusader 9
Prerequisite: Three Devoted Spirit maneuvers
Initiation Action: 1 Standard Action
Range: Melee Attack
Target: One Creature

As your enemy reels from your mighty blow, an ally nearby is simultaneously healed and cleansed of its wounds by the power of your faith.

You make a single attack against an enemy who's alignment has at least one component different from yours. If you hit, you or an ally with 10 feet is healed, as per the heal spell.
