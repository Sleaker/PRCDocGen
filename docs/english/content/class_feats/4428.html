:: Brawler Fists ::

Type of Feat: Class Specific
Prerequisite: Brawler Level 1
Specifics: Your fists grow in striking power as you gain levels. In addition, they strike as magical weapons after level 6, allowing you to penetrate certain defenses. You cannot strike with your fist proficiently if you are using a shield.

Level:
1: 1d6
6: 1d8 (+1 enhancement)
12: 1d10 (+2 enhancement)
18: 2d6 (+3 enhancement)
24: 2d8 (+4 enhancement)
30: 2d10 (+5 enhancement)
36: 3d8 (+6 enhancement)
