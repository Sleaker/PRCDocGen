:: Wilder Ectoplasmic Cocoon ::

Metacreativity
Level: Shaper 3
Manifesting Time: 1 standard action
Range: Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./ level)
Target: One Medium or smaller creature
Duration: 1 round/level
Saving Throw: Reflex negates
Power Resistance: No
Power Points: 5
Metapsionics: Extend, Twin

You draw writhing strands of ectoplasm from the Astral Plane that wrap up the subject like a mummy. The subject can still breathe but is otherwise helpless, unable to see outside the cocoon, speak, or take any physical actions. The subject's nostrils are clear (air passes through the cocoon normally).

Augment: You can augment this power in one or both of the following ways.
1. For every 2 additional power points you spend, this power's save DC increases by 1.
2. For every 2 additional power points you spend, this power can affect a target one size category larger.
