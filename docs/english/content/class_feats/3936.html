:: Breath Weapon ::

Type of Feat: Class
Prerequisite: Talon of Tiamat 1

You gain a breath weapon that can be used once every 1d4 rounds. As your level in the Talon of Tiamat class rises, you gain access to the following breaths:

1: 3d6 30' cone of cold
3: 8d4 60' line of acid
5: 10d6 30' cone of acidic gas
7: 12d8 60' line of lightning
9: 14d8 30' cone of fire

Subradial entries:

Breath Weapon, Cone of Cold
Breath Weapon, Line of Acid
Breath Weapon, Cone of Acid
Breath Weapon, Line of Electricity
Breath Weapon, Cone of Fire
