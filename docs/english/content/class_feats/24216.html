:: Mad Certainty ::

Type of Feat: Class
Prerequisite: Alienist 4

Constantly dwelling on entities beyond the reach of normal space and time is mentally corrosive, and the alienist's mind begins to fracture. She develops a phobia against a specified kind of creature, suffering a -2 penalty to saving throws, attack rolls, and Charisma based skill and ability checks in regard to these creatures.

Feat Replaced By:

Insane Certainty
