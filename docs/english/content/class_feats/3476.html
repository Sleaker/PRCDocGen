:: Death Knell ::

Type of Feat: Class
Prerequisite: Anti-Paladin.
Specifics: an Anti-Paladin may use Death knell as a spell-like ability. If the target has less than 10 hp and fails a will save vs. DC of 12 + Wis Modifier, the target dies and the caster gains +2 Str, +1d8 HP, and +1 caster level for a number of turns equal to caster level.
Use: Selected.

Feat Replaced By:

Death Knell
