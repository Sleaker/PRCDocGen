:: Researched Epic Spell: Momento Mori ::

School: Necromancy [Death]
Components: None
Range: Medium
Target: One living creature
Duration: Instantaneous
Saving Throw: Fortitude partial (see text)
Spell Resistance: Yes

Your passing thought connotes death for the target. You will the target dead without a word or gesture. Your bloodthirsty wish snuffs out the life force of a creature of less than 50 hit dice, killing it instantly. The subject is entitled to a Fortitude saving throw (DC 30 + relevant ability modifier) to have a chance of surviving the attack. If the save is successful, the target instead takes 3d6+20 points of damage.
