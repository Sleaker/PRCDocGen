:: Warblade Press The Advantage ::

White Raven (Stance)
Level: Crusader 5, Warblade 5
Prerequisite: Two White Raven Maneuvers
Initiation Action: 1 Swift Action
Range: Personal
Target: You
Duration: Stance

You shift across the battlefield, timing your movements precisely to avoid any attacks from your foe.

You gain a 5 foot increase to your land speed.
