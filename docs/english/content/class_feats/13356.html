:: Mystic Undeath's Eternal Foe ::

Caster Level(s): Cleric 9
Innate Level: 9
School: Abjuration
Descriptor(s):
Component(s): Verbal, Somatic
Range: Long
Area of Effect / Target: Medium
Duration: 1 round / level
Additional Counter Spells:
Save: None
Spell Resistance: No

All allies in the area of effect will receive the following bonuses: immunity to negative damage, immunity to level/energy drain, immunity to ability score decreases, immunity to poisons, immunity to diseases and a +4 deflection bonus to AC.
