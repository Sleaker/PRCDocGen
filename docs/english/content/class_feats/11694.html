Twilight Luck
Abjuration [Good]
Level: Sanctified 1
Components: V
Casting Time: 1 standard action
Range: Touch
Target: One non-evil creature touched
Duration: 1 minute/level
Saving Throw: None
Spell Resistance: Yes (harmless)

By means of this spell, the caster can impart the luck of the fey to one non-evil being. The target gains a +1 luck bonus on all saving throws for the duration of the spell.

Casters of evil alignment cannot cast Sanctified spells.
