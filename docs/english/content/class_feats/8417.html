:: Warblade Blistering Flourish ::

Desert Wind (Strike)
Level: Swordsage 1
Initiation Action: 1 Standard Action
Range: 30ft.
Area: 30-ft burst
Duration: 1 Minute

Your weapons bursts into flames as you twirl it over your head. With a flourish, you cause the fire to explode with a blinding flash.

All creatures other than you in the area must make a Fortitude save vs 11 + Wisdom modifier or be dazzled.
This is a supernatural maneuver.
