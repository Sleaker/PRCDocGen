:: Archivist Control Undead ::

Caster Level(s): Cleric 6, Wizard / Sorcerer 7
Innate Level: 7
School: Necromancy
Descriptor(s):
Component(s): Verbal, Somatic
Range: Short
Area of Effect / Target: Single
Duration: 1 Hour / Level
Additional Counter Spells: Sunbeam
Save: Will Negates
Spell Resistance: Yes

A single undead creature with up to 2 Hit Dice per caster level falls under the caster's control unless it makes a successful Will save.
