:: Crusader Boulder Roll ::

Stone Dragon (Strike)
Level: Crusader 4, Swordsage 4, Warblade 4
Prerequisite: Two Stone Dragon maneuvers
Initiation Action: 1 Standard Action
Range: Melee Attack
Target: One Creature

Like a boulder tumbling down the mountain, you slam through your enemies.

You make an overrun attempt against each creature in a line with a +4 bonus. You do not provoke attacks of opportunity, and if you fail, you are not knocked prone, you simply halt.
