:: Scro ::


Scro are a race that has only recently begun to reappear in Wildspace. They are highly militaristic and violent. After their defeat in the First Unhuman Wars by the Elven spelljammer fleet, they sequestered themselves on a far crystal sphere and are now on a holy crusade to drive all non-scro out of Wildspace. Physically they resemble orcs, standing tall and straight, with skin colors ranging from gray to burnt orange, to moss green, or even jet black or albino.

Scro Ability Adjustments: +4 Str, +2 Dex, +2 Con, -2 Cha.
Favored Class (Fighter): A multiclass scro's fighter class does not count when determining whether he suffers an XP penalty for multiclassing.

Special Abilities:

- Darkvision: Scro are able to see in the dark.
- Racial Training: Scro are proficient in light armor and simple weapons.
- Orcish: Gray Orcs are considered Orcish for the purpose of spells.
