:: Baaz Draconian ::


Baaz Draconians are a race of dragon-like humanoids created originally from the eggs of brass dragons and are the smallest and most common type of draconian. Baaz typically serve as soldiers, and are historically mistreated by other draconian breeds. The arrival of female Baaz draconians is beginning to change this, female Baaz being superb organizers by nature. Male baaz on the other hand tend to be shallow, selfish and sadistic, or at the very least undisciplined. They do not possess any magical powers. When a Baaz is killed, its body turns into stone, often trapping the weapon used to kill it. After a short time passes, the stone body will crumble on its own and the trapped weapon will then be released, unharmed.

Bozak Draconian Ability Adjustments: +2 Con, -2 Int, -2 Wis.
Favored Class(Fighter): A multiclass baaz's fighter class does not count when determining whether he suffers an XP penalty for multiclassing.

Special Abilities:

- 30' base speed
- Darkvision: Allows them to see in the dark.
- Spell Resistance: Baaz have spell resistance equal to 8 + their level.
- Natural Armor: +2 racial bonus to Natural Armor.
- Natural Weapons: Has a bite attack for 1d4 and 2 claw attacks for 1d4 each.
- Immunities: Immune to sleep, paralyzation, and disease.
- Death Throes: When a baaz dies, the corpse turns to stone around the weapon that struck the killing blow, trapping it. After a time, the corpse turns to dust, leaving the weapon unharmed.
- Proficiencies: Proficient with all simple weapons.
- Dragon: Baaz Draconians count as Dragons for the purpose of spells.
