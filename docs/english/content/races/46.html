:: Tinker Gnome ::


Tinker gnomes are the tinkers and inventors of Krynn. Their mind is always awhirl with technological dreams and ideas. Upon reaching adulthood, every gnome takes up a Life Quest, a goal that they strive towards throughout their entire life. They are specific and usually outlandish; it's not uncommon for a given Life Quest to be passed through several generations of a family. Gnomes are typically too focused on their plans and devices to think much of social graces; it's not that they mean to be rude, the thought just doesn't occur to them. They also believe that the more complicated it is, the better it is. Physically, they are like their cousins on other worlds: 3' tall humanoids with teak-colored skin, blue or violet eyes, and long white hair. Males usually grow a extremely curly beard and moustache. As a race they tend to speak extremely fast, individual words almost seeming to run together.

Rock Gnomish Ability Adjustments: +2 Dex, +2 Int, -2 Str, -2 Wis.
Favored Class (Any): A multiclass tinker gnome's highest class does not count when determining whether he suffers an XP penalty for multiclassing.

Special Abilities:

- Small Stature
+1 size bonus to attack rolls.
+1 size bonus to AC.
+4 size bonus to Hide checks.
- Hardiness vs. Illusions: +2 racial bonus on saving throws against mind-affecting spells.
- Offensive Training vs. Reptilians: +1 racial bonus on attack rolls against reptilian humanoids.
- Offensive Training vs. Goblinoids: +1 racial bonus on attack rolls against goblinoids (goblins, hobgoblins, and bugbears).
- Defensive Training vs. Giants: +4 dodge bonus to AC against giants.
- Skill Affinity (Listen): +2 racial bonus to Listen checks.
- Skill Affinity (Concentration): +2 racial bonus to Concentration checks.
- Spell Focus (Illusion): Rock Gnomes add +2 DC to any Illusion spells they cast. This is identical to the feat.
- Low-Light Vision: Allows them to see better than normal in the dark.
- Life Quest: Tinker gnomes are extremely hard to distract from their Life Quest, which grants them a +2 to will saves.
- Guilds: Every gnome chooses a guild when they choose a Life Quest. This guild will not change for the life of the gnome.
Craft Guild gnomes gain a +2 to all crafting skill checks.
Tech Guild gnomes gain a +1 to all crafting and Lore checks.
Sage Guild gnomes gain a +2 to all Lore checks.
- Gnomish: Tinker Gnomes count as Gnomes for the purpose of spells.
