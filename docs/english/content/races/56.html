:: Krynn Minotaur ::


Minotaurs from the world of Krynn are an honorable race. They are a warrior culture, and promote bravery, glory, and honor. if they are fighting for a cause they believe in, they will dedicate themselves to that cause. They are also natural sailors. They believe that they are superior to all other races, though they respect dwarves for their ruggedness and humans for their sailing ability and military skills. Physically they are smaller than their cousins on other worlds, between 6'6" and 7' tall typically, though just as powerfully built, relatively. They tend to dress austerely, preferring a simple harness and skirt or kilt, though nobles will occasionally wear robes.

Minotaur Ability Adjustments: +4 Str, -2 Dex, -2 Int, -2 Cha
Favored Class (Fighter): A multiclass Krynn minotaur's fighter class does not count when determining whether he suffers an XP penalty for multiclassing.

Special Abilities:

- Minotaur Scent: +4 racial bonus to Search, Spot and Listen checks.
- Skill Affinity(Intimidate): +2 racial bonus to Intimidate checks.
- Natural Armor Bonus: Racial natural armor bonus of 2.
- Natural Weapon: Minotaurs can make an unarmed gore attack with their horns.
- Powerful Charge: Minotaurs can make a charging gore attack at +2 to hit, -2 to AC for an additional 4d6+6 damage.
- Monstrous: Krynn Minotaurs count as Monstrous Humanoids for the purpose of spells.
