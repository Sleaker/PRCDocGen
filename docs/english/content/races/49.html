:: Kender ::


Kender are like the children or Krynn. They have short attention spans, intense curiosity, and a fearlessness that leads them into and out of more troubles than a dragon could count. You never want to hear a kender say "Oops." They will leave no chest unlocked, and no shiny object unhandled. They have a tendency to pick up things that don't belong to them, but don't consider it stealing, and dislike thieves as much as anyone else. They instead consider it borrowing, as they fully intend to return it; just with so much going on in the world to see, they sometimes forget for a long time. They are creatures of extremes, and other creatures tend to get caught up in their moods. They are also horrible at keeping secrets. Physically, kender are similar to halflings of other worlds. They stand between 3' and 4', rarely going up to 5'. Their hair runs the gamut form light brown and blond, to coppery red, with styles ranging from short-cropped to long. Their eyes are usually a blue or hazel color, and their clothes are typically flamboyant. Aware of the importance of first impressions, when a kender first meets someone new, their first instinct is to brush themselves off, straighten their clothes, and offer a hand to the new person.

Kender Ability Adjustments: +2 Dex, -2 Str, -2 Wis.
Favored Class (Rogue): A multiclass kender's rogue class does not count when determining whether he suffers an XP penalty for multiclassing.

Special Abilities:

- Small Stature:
+1 size bonus to attack rolls.
+1 size bonus to AC.
+4 size bonus to Hide checks.
- Skill Affinity (Spot): +2 racial bonus to Spot checks.
- Skill Affinity (Open Lock): +2 racial bonus to Open Lock checks.
- Skill Affinity (Pick Pocket): +2 racial bonus to Pick Pocket checks.
- Lucky: +1 luck bonus to all saving throws.
- Fearless: Kender are immune to spells and effects of the 'Fear' subtype.
- Lack of Focus: -4 racial penalty to Concentration checks.
- Kender Taunting: +4 racial bonus to Bluff and Taunt.
- Halfling: Kender are considered Halflings for the purpose of spells.

Special: Kender are small creatures and as such they can never use the following Large weapons: bastard sword, dire mace, two-bladed sword, double axe, greataxe, greatsword, halberd, heavy flail, quarterstaff, scythe and spear.
