:: Bozak Draconian ::


Bozak Draconians are a race of dragon-like humanoids created originally from the eggs of bronze dragons. They are skilled magic users and are typically clerics. They take pride in knowing a variety of spells, and Bozaks are also good with languages (during his twenty-year stay in the Kharolis mountains, a Bozak named Kang picked up a large working vocabulary in Dwarven, while Slith, Kang's Sivak second-in-command, learned very little Dwarven in the equivalent span of time). Bozaks are physically about the same size as Kapak draconians, but tend to be more intelligent; nevertheless, they are also proficient melee combatants and are natural tacticians and strategists. When a Bozak is killed, its scales shrivel and its bones are revealed; the bones then explode.

Bozak Draconian Ability Adjustments: +2 Str, +2 Int, +2 Cha.
Favored Class (Sorcerer): A multiclass bozak's sorcerer class does not count when determining whether he suffers an XP penalty for multiclassing.

Special Abilities:

- 30' base speed
- Darkvision: Allows them to see in the dark.
- Spell Resistance: Bozaks have spell resistance equal to 14 + their level.
- Natural Armor: +8 racial bonus to Natural Armor.
- Natural Weapons: Has a bite attack for 1d6 and 2 claw attacks for 1d4 each.
- Immunities: Immune to sleep, paralyzation, and disease.
- Death Throes: When a bozak dies, he explodes in a cloud of dust and bone, dealing 1d6 damage to everything within 10'. A reflex save vs 10 + Con reduces the damage by half.
- Proficiencies: Proficient with light armor, shields, all simple weapons, longsword, longbow, and shortsword.
- Dragon: Bozak Draconians count as Dragons for the purpose of spells.
