:: Dark Dwarf ::


Native to the world of Krynn, these dwarves prefer to live completely in darkness, and are considered mad by other Krynn dwarves. They have a reputation for evil and murderous acts, and have betrayed their own kind in the past, but still they claim loyalty to the High Thane and the dwarven race. Physically they are extremely fair, with the occasional albino being born to them.

Dark Dwarf Ability Adjustments: +2 Con, -4 Cha.
Favored Class (Rogue): A multiclass dark dwarf's rogue class does not count when determining whether he suffers an XP penalty for multiclassing.

- Stonecunning: +2 racial bonus on Search checks made in subterranean areas.
- Darkvision: Dark Dwarves are able to see in the dark.
- Hardiness vs. Poisons: +2 racial bonus on saving throws against poisons.
- Hardiness vs. Spells: +2 racial bonus on saving throws against spells.
- Offensive Training vs. Orcs: +1 racial bonus on attack rolls against orcs.
- Offensive Training vs. Goblinoids: +1 racial bonus on attack rolls against goblinoids.
- Defensive Training vs. Giants: +4 dodge bonus to AC against giants.
- Skill Affinity (Lore): +2 racial bonus to Lore checks.
- Skill Affinity (Hide): +2 racial bonus to Hide.
- Skill Affinity (Move Silently): +2 racial bonus to Move Silently.
- Skill Affinity (Craft): +2 racial bonus to Craft Armor and Craft Weapon checks.
- Skill Affinity (Appraise): +2 racial bonus to Appraise checks.
- Skill Affinity (Listen): +2 racial bonus to Listen checks.
- Light Sensitivity: Dark Dwarves suffer -2 penalty to attack rolls, saves and checks while operating in bright light.
- Dwarven: Dark Dwarves count as Dwarves for the purpose of spells.
