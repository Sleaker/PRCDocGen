:: Epic Weapon Focus (unarmed strike) ::

Type of Feat: Combat
Prerequisite: 21st level, Weapon Focus with chosen weapon.
Specifics: The character gains a +2 bonus to all attack rolls with the chosen weapon.
Use: Automatic.

Requires All of These Feats:

Weapon Focus (unarmed strike)

Required For the Following Feats:

Epic Weapon Specialization (unarmed strike)
