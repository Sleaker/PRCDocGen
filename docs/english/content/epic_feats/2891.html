:: Zone Of Animation ::

Type of Feat: Epic
Prerequisite: Turn Undead, 25 Charisma, Undead Mastery
Specifics: You can use a rebuke or command undead attempt to animate corpses. You animate a total number of HD of undead equal to the number of undead that would be commanded by your result. You cant animate more undead with any single attempt than the maximum number you can command (including any undead already under your command). These undead are automatically under your command, though your normal limit of commanded undead still applies
Use: Selected

Requires All of These Feats:

Undead Mastery
