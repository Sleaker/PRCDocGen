:: Metamagic Catalyst ::


Level: Evolving Mind 5
Range: 60 feet
Target: One Creature
Duration: 5 Rounds or 1 round
Spell Resistance: Yes
Save: None
Metautterances: Extend, Empower

Normal: Your ally experiences a tremendous rejuvenation as healing energy soothes her wounds.
Your ally gains regeneration +15.
Reverse: Massive rents and tears appear on the body of your foe.
Your foe takes a 8d6 damage, and 8d6 damage the next round if you concentrate. Concentration involves doing nothing more strenuous than moving.

Subradial entries:

Metamagic Catalyst, Empower
Metamagic Catalyst, Extend
Metamagic Catalyst, Maximize
