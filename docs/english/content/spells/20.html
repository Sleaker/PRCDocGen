:: Clairaudience / Clairvoyance ::


Divination (Scrying)
Level: Bard 3, Sorc/Wiz 3
Components: V, S
Casting Time: 1 standard action
Range: Special
Effect: See text
Duration: 1 Min/level
Saving Throw: None.
Spell Resistance: No.

You create a magical sensor that allows you to spy on creatures that are within the area with you.

Valid targets are any creatures within the same area as the caster.
