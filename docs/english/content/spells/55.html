:: Feeblemind ::


Caster Level(s): Wizard / Sorcerer 5
Innate Level: 5
School: Divination
Descriptor(s): Mind-Affecting
Component(s): Verbal, Somatic
Range: Medium
Area of Effect / Target: Single
Duration: 1 Round / 2 Levels
Additional Counter Spells: Heal
Save: Will Negates
Spell Resistance: Yes

With a successful ranged touch attack, the caster does 1d4 points of Intelligence damage to the creature for every 4 caster levels.
