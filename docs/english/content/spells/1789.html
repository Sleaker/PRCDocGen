:: Scare ::


Necromancy [Fear, Mind-Affecting]
Level: Brd 2, Sor/Wiz 2
Components: V, S, M
Casting Time: 1 standard action
Range: Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
Targets: One living creature per three levels, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart
Duration: 1 round/level or 1 round
Saving Throw: Will partial
Spell Resistance: Yes

This spell functions like cause fear, except that it causes all targeted creatures of less than 6 HD to become frightened. Creatures that make their saving throw are shaken.

Material Component: A bit of bone from an undead skeleton, zombie, ghoul, ghast, or mummy.
