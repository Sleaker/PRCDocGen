:: Magic Fang ::


Caster Level(s): Druid 1, Ranger 1
Innate Level: 1
School: Transmutation
Descriptor(s):
Component(s): Verbal, Somatic
Range: Personal
Area of Effect / Target: Caster
Duration: 1 minute / level
Additional Counter Spells:
Save: None
Spell Resistance: No

This spell strengthens the natural attacks of the target, giving it +1 to hit and +1 to damage. It also grants the creature the ability to strike as if it were a +1 weapon (so it can bypass other creature's damage reduction).
