:: Arrowstorm ::


Arrow Storm
Transmutation
Level: Ranger 3
Components: V
Casting Time: 1 swift action
Range: Personal
Target: You
Duration: 1 round

After casting arrow storm, you use a full-round action to make one ranged attack with a bow with which you are proficient against every foe within range. You can attack a maximum number of individual targets equal to your character level.
