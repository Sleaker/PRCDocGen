:: Greater Scrying ::


Divination (Scrying)
Level: Bard 6, Cleric 7, Druid 7, Sorc/Wiz 7
Components: V, S
Casting Time: 1 standard action
Range: Special
Effect: See text
Duration: 1 Hour/level
Saving Throw: Will negates
Spell Resistance: Yes

You can see and hear some creature, which may be at any distance. If the subject succeeds on a Will save, the scrying attempt simply fails. If the save fails, you can see and hear the subject and the subject's immediate surroundings. Detect Chaos, Good, Evil, or Law may be cast while Scrying. All other spells will fail.

Valid targets are any monster within the same area as the caster, or any PC.
