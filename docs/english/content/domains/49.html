:: Halfling ::


Type of Feat: Domain
Prerequisite: Access to the Halfling domain.
Granted Power: You gain the ability to add your Charisma Modifier to your Jump, Hide, and Move Silently checks. It can be used once a day and lasts for 10 minutes.
Bonus Spells: The cleric gains access to the following spells at the specified spell level: Camouflage (1), Cat's Grace (2), Freedom of Movement (3), Improved Invisibility (6)
Use: Once/Day.
