:: Magic ::


Magic Domain
Clerics who take the Magic domain gain access to an improved list of bonus spells.
Bonus Spells
The cleric gains access to the following spells at the specified spell level: Mage Armor (1), Melf's Acid Arrow (2), Negative Energy Burst (3), Stoneskin (4), Ice Storm (5).
