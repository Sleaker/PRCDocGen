:: Family ::


Type of Feat: Domain
Prerequisite: Access to Family domain.
Granted Power: As a free action, you may protect a number of creatures equal to your Charisma Modifier (minimum 1) with a +4 bonus to AC. This lasts 1 round per level.
Bonus Spells: The cleric gains access to the following spells at the specified spell level: Divine Protection (1), Legion's Shield of Faith (3), Protection From Spells (7)
Use: Once/Day.
