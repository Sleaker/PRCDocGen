:: Hatred ::


Type of Feat: Domain
Prerequisite: Access to Hatred domain.
Granted Power: Once per day as a free action, choose 1 race. You gain +2 Attack, Saving Throw, and Armour Class vs that race. This lasts for 1 minute.
Bonus Spells: The cleric gains access to the following spells at the specified spell level: Bestow Curse (2), Blood Frenzy (3), Righteous Might (4), Wail of the Banshee (9)
Use: Once/Day.
