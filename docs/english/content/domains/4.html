:: Destruction ::


Type of Feat: Domain
Prerequisite: Access to the Destruction domain.
Granted Power: Once per day, you may make a single melee attack with a +4 bonus to attack, and a damage bonus equal to your cleric level.
Bonus Spells: The cleric gains access to the following spells at the specified spell level: Slay Living (5), Crumble (6), Disintegrate (7), Implosion (8)
Use: Automatic.
