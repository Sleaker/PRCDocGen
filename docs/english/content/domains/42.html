:: Charm ::


Type of Feat: Domain
Prerequisite: Access to Charm domain.
Granted Power: You may boost your Charisma score by +4 once per day. This boost lasts for 1 minute.
Bonus Spells: The cleric gains access to the following spells at the specified spell level: Charm Monster (3), Dominate Person (5), Mass Charm (7), Dominate Monster (9)
Use: Once/Day.
