:: Spell Power (Ex) ::

Type of Feat: Class specific
Prerequisite: Duskblade level 6
Starting at 6th level, a Duskblade can more easily overcome the spell resistance of any opponent he successfully injures with a melee attack. If he has injured an opponent with a melee attack, he gains a +2 bonus on his caster level check to overcome spell resistance for the remainder of the encounter. This bonus increases by 1 every five levels afterwards, with an additional increase at level 18 and 38.
