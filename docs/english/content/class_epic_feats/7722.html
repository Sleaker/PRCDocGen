:: Epic Fiendish Resilience III ::

Type of Feat: Epic
Prerequisite: Fiendish Resilience that grants Fast Healing 5.
Specifics: When you use your fiendish resilience ability, you gain fast healing 25 for 5 minutes.
You can gain feat multiple times. Each time you take the feat, you gain an additional use of the fiendish resilience ability, and the fast healing granted by the feat increases by 5.
Use: Automatic.

Requires All of These Feats:

Epic Fiendish Resilience II
Fiendish Resilience

Feat Replaced By:

Epic Fiendish Resilience IV

Required For the Following Feats:

Epic Fiendish Resilience IV
