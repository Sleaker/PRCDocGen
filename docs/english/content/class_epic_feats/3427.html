:: Epic Mystic Theurge ::

Though your knowledge of all matters arcane and divine is second to none, still your thirst for power drives you forward. Your search for the deep secrets of magic continues to lead you to every corner of this world and beyond.

Hit Die: d4.
Skill Points at Each Additional Level: 2+Int Modifier.
Bonus Feats: The Epic Mystic Theurge gains a bonus feat every four levels after 10th.

Epic Mystic Theurge Bonus Feats:
Automatic Quicken Spell, Automatic Silent Spell, Automatic Still Spell, Energy Resistance, Epic Spell Focus, Epic Spell Penetration, Improved Combat Casting, Planar Turning, Epic Spells.
