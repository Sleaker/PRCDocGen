:: Epic Celestial Companion (Ex) ::

Type: Class
Prerequisite: Healer level 24
The Epic Healer's Celestial Companion continues to increase in power. Starting at level 24 and if the healer's effective level for the companion is 21 or higher, the companion gains +2 bonus Hit Dice, it's natural armor increases by +2, and its Strength, Dexterity, and Intelligence increase by +1. The Companion's spell resistance equals the Healer's effective level +5.
