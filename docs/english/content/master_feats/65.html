:: Major Aura ::


Type of Feat: Class Specific
Prerequisite: Marshal
Specifics: When a Major Aura is active, it grants a bonus to certain skills or abilities based on your level. This bonus is granted to you, and all allies within 60 feet. While you learn new Auras as you level, you may only have 1 Major Aura active at any given time.
Use: Activated.
