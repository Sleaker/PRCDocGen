:: Dragon Heritage ::


Type of Feat: Draconic
Prerequisite: Sorcerer 1, or Dragontouched.

You have a greater connection with your draconic bloodline than others of your kind. You gain the dragonblood subtype. In addition, choose a dragon type. You gain a +2 to a specific skill based on the type of dragon chosen. You also qualify to take other Draconic feats.
