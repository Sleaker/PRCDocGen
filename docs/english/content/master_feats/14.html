:: Epic Spell ::


Epic Spellcasters begin to understand how magic really works. Through application of formidable intellect, vast wisdom, or sheer force of personality, a spellcaster can manipulate cosmic and personal energy directly. Epic spells are neither considered arcane, nor divine, thus all spellcasting classes capable of casting 9th level spells can learn how to cast them.

Each epic spell known to a character can be cast only once per day.

For a list and description of epic spells available in Hordes of the Underdark, please refer to your manual.
