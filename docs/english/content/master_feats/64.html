:: Minor Aura ::


Type of Feat: Class Specific
Prerequisite: Marshal
Specifics: When a Minor Aura is active, it grants a bonus to certain skills or abilities equal to your Charisma bonus. This bonus is granted to you, and all allies within 60 feet. While you learn new Auras as you level, you may only have 1 Minor Aura active at any given time.
Use: Activated.
