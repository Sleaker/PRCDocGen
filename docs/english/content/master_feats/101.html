:: Minor Oozy Touch ::


Type of Feat: Class Specific
Prerequisite: Oozemaster Level 1
Specifics: The Oozemaster's hands can secrete a specific kind of ooze. The character may, as a full attack action, make a melee touch attack that has the effect listed for that kind of ooze on the table below. The Oozemaster can use this ability as often as desired. At 3rd level, he may choose one additional oozy minor oozy touch.
Use: Selected.

Minor Oozy Touch(Brown Mold)
Minor Oozy Touch(Gray Ooze)
Minor Oozy Touch(Ochre Jelly)
Minor Oozy Touch(Phosphorescent Fungus)
