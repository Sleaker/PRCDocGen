:: Totem Dragon ::


When you become a dragon shaman, you must select a metallic or chromatic dragon type that you relate to and draw your other abilities from. This dragon should be within one step of your alignment.

Note: You can only select one totem. Selecting more than one will force you to relevel. If you select more than one at level 1 you will be unable to level.
