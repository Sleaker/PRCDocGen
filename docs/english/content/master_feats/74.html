:: Major Aura (Draconic) ::


In place of a Major Aura, Marshals can learn Draconic Auras. The aura bonus grows at the same rate as the major auras, and can be run alongside existing auras. Only one Draconic Aura can be active at any time, unless the feat "Double Draconic Aura" is taken.
