:: Metabreath Feats ::


This series of feats allows you to enhance a breath weapon at the cost of increasing the recharge time. Thus, these feats are only usable on breath weapons with a recharge time.

Clinging Breath
Enlarge Breath
Heighten Breath
Lingering Breath
Maximize Breath
Recover Breath
Shape Breath
Spreading Breath
Tempest Breath
