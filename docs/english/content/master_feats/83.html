:: Dragon Augmentation ::


Type of Feat: Class
Prerequisite: Diamond Dragon Level 1

Learning to think the way dragons think is more than a matter of mental training. Dragons are extremely fit creatures, and a psionic character must begin to match that fitness as part of learning their mental habits.

Effect: At 1st, 5th, and 10th levels a Diamond Dragon may choose to add a +1 to either Strength, Dexterity, or Constitution.
